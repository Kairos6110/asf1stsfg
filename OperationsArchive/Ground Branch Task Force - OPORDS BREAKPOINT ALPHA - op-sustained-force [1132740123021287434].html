Operation Sustained Force

HAHO southwest of Dark Wood, liberate Dunback Farm, Forsyth Farm, and Burnside Orchard, seize the food trucks Sentinel has been diverting, convoy all supplies to NC-19, and coordinate the Coral boat pickup before exfil to HQ.

Launch Site
Erewhon / sundown / green kit
Insertion
Helicopter to altitude, HAHO to LZ southwest of Dark Wood
Convoy Destination
Site NC-19 food-truck handoff
POC Callsign
Coral

Situation

Sentinel units have seized civilian farms north of New Stirling and are diverting loaded food trucks to undisclosed locations. That theft is draining the regional resistance cache and weakening Outcast sustainment.

The mission is to retake all three farms, neutralize targets of opportunity, and move the recovered food trucks to NC-19 for Outcast pickup.

Execution

Jump HAHO to the LZ, move farm-to-farm in sequence, neutralize resistance, collect intel, secure each truck, then convoy the three vehicles to NC-19 and contact Coral for the boat handoff.

  • Farm ClearsRetake Dunback Farm, Forsyth Farm, and Burnside Orchard and secure each truck in turn.
  • NC-19 DeliveryConvoy all recovered supplies to November Charlie One Niner and prep the load for transport.
  • Coral LinkupRadio Coral for pickup ETA, hold security until Outcasts arrive, then exfil or fast-travel RTB.

TOO Clearance

Handle targets of opportunity during each farm clear, but keep the priority on preserving the food trucks and preventing supply loss.

Radio Script

"Coral, this is Dagger One, Sierra safely delivered to NC-19 at Grid 256840. Contents are being prepped for transport, advise ETA, over."

Extraction

Once Coral confirms the pickup and Outcasts arrive, mission is complete. RTB to Erewhon or call extract based on command preference.
