Operation Crypt

A late-afternoon helicopter package from South Cape Station will hit four Northeast Liberty sites tied to Sentinel's organ-trafficking pipeline while the British NCA raids the London market operating under the alias Golden Goose. The team must establish PID, eliminate the commanders, radio operators, and bagmen running the local network, and collect actionable intelligence before any alert lets the suspects scatter.

Situation

Intelligence links a London-based organ market to Sentinel harvesting operations on Auroa. Operating under the alias Golden Goose, the network sells organs taken post-mortem from Outcasts captured during Sentinel raids, then ships them overseas through a concealed medical-processing chain. Intercepted traffic confirms the people running that network are motivated by major financial returns and are actively coordinating with overseas buyers.

The NCA will strike the London market at the same time this team hits Cryohope, the Cryogenization Center, Eternity, and Auroa Hospital in Northeast Liberty. The intent is a synchronized dismantling of both ends of the pipeline, paired with enough site exploitation to drive follow-on targeting.

Execution

The team deploys by helicopter in late afternoon, lands south of Cryohope, and then clears each marked site on foot. At every objective, PID comes first, followed by the elimination of high-ranking operators and the collection of records, devices, or documents. Civilians at the marked locations are suspected collaborators, so hidden weapons remain a concern even when personnel present as noncombatants.

  • AlphaClear Cryohope, identify leadership personnel, and seize all intelligence tied to local processing and shipment management.
  • BravoPush to the Cryogenization Center and repeat the PID, leadership kill/capture, and site-exploitation process.
  • CharlieHit Eternity, account for the large guard presence and possible underground space, and pull all exploitable material before anyone flees.
  • DeltaSecure Auroa Hospital, eliminate the command cell, document the execution evidence, and extract using the on-site helicopter if viable.

Enemy Presence

Current estimates indicate 10 tangos at Cryohope, 9 at the Cryogenization Center, 15+ at Eternity with a possible underground section on the right side, and a platoon-sized element of 25+ at Auroa Hospital.

Cleaner Threat

Sentinel cleaners may appear in civilian clothing at the target sites. Maintain weapon and hand awareness during PID because concealed weapons remain a realistic threat.

External Link

NCA footage from London positively identifies a missing Outcast inside the overseas organ market, while local footage shows another execution at Auroa Hospital 17 hours before the briefing. Those two references reinforce the direct Liberty-to-London pipeline.
