Situation

Cyclops tracked a special-unit transport specialist moving Murmur drone shipments from island factories to Wolves camps and finally to a secret meeting at Skell Technology Head Office.

Cyclops had to relocate after only two ISR captures due to increased radar activity, but the window is good enough to capture the transport specialist alive and exploit his unit's logistics network.

Execution

Attack from the roof, clear down fast, interrogate the transport specialist in the lot, destroy the staged Murmur shipment, then use his phone lead to raid Auroa Hospital, capture the lieutenant, and free prisoners at Detention Center.
