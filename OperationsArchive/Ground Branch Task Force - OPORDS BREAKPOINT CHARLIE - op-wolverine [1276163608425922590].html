Operation Wolverine

Enemy forces have launched from a forward operating base tied to the Auroa Weather Station and are holding hostages while attempting to coordinate with the MARAUDERS cell. Team Wolverine must cut the quick reaction force relay, wipe the supporting FOB, and reach the weather station fast enough to free the captives before negotiations turn into a handoff or execution.

FOB South Cape Station FOB
Step-Off 1900 hours
Insertion HALO in white kits
Force Mix DEMO and OVERWATCH elements attached

Situation

The situation in the mountainous south-central Auroa region is unstable and escalating. REAPER-1-3 ISR confirms the enemy is holding multiple hostages at the Auroa Weather Station while coordinating with the MARAUDERS terrorist cell for a possible transfer.

The team must first blind and break the enemy's quick reaction capability, then destroy the supporting FOB before pushing the final hostage rescue. Targets of opportunity across the AO are authorized, and no negotiation is to be entertained with the hostile cell.

Execution

  • Insert HALO from South Cape Station in white kits and move on foot toward Alpha and Bravo.
  • Mobility Acquire vehicles inside the AO only as needed after the initial infiltration phase.
  • Tempo Move with speed and precision to keep the weather station blind to the assault chain.
  • End After Delta, remain on site to protect civilians until GUARDIAN arrives for relocation and conduct debrief on site.

Priority Intel

Threat link: Weather Station captors are negotiating with MARAUDERS.

The relay at the mine and the QRF at Nugget Cliff are the key interruption points. If they stay live, the weather station can harden before Team Wolverine reaches the hostage package.

Mission End State

The enemy relay and QRF are destroyed, Red Ferret is rendered unusable, hostages are recovered alive, and civilians remain under Team Wolverine protection until GUARDIAN completes the relocation handoff.

Objectives

Alpha

Mine Relay Site

Rig the communications relay antenna with C4 so the enemy loses the ability to warn the weather station in time.

Bravo

Nugget Cliff

Detonate the relay charges and assault the enemy quick reaction force simultaneously before they can react or reinforce.

Charlie

Outpost Red Ferret

Secure the outpost, liberate the POW, and demolish the enemy's equipment, weapons, and vehicles with controlled charges.

Delta

Auroa Weather Station

Neutralize all hostiles fast enough to prevent hostage casualties, then establish contact with GUARDIAN for civilian relocation.
