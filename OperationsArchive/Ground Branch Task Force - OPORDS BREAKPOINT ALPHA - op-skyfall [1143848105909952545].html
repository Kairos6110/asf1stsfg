Operation Skyfall

Deploy from South Cape Station, fly high over SAM coverage, HALO southeast of Arrow Testing Zone, neutralize Azrael drones at Alpha, Bravo, and Delta, stop the Grandview Mansion abduction cell, and hold their leader alive for OXIDE pickup.

Parameters

Insertion

Situation

Azrael strikes across Auroa escalated beyond control last night around 0200 when Sentinel hit Outcasts and non-combatants. OR-4 estimates at least fifteen casualties while ground reconnaissance is still building the full picture.

The mission is to neutralize all Azraels and hostiles at three primary sites while also stopping the Sentinel squad abducting civilians at Grandview Mansion.

Execution

Fly north from South Cape Station at elevated altitude to avoid confirmed SAM coverage, HALO to the LZ, move through Alpha and Bravo, capture the Charlie leader alive, then finish Delta and exfil from the Maintenance Area.

  • Alpha / BravoClear Arrow Testing Zone and Assembly Hall Omega Two and destroy all Azrael drones.
  • CharlieEliminate the abduction squad at Grandview Mansion, interrogate their leader, then leave him secured for OXIDE.
  • DeltaClear the Maintenance Area, neutralize remaining Azraels, and exfil.

Air Threat

Most drones are grounded, but potential Azrael fly-bys remain possible overhead. Stay ready to prosecute air threats fast.

Civilian Rescue

Grandview Mansion is tied to an active civilian-abduction cell. Control the site quickly and preserve the leader for questioning.

Exfil

Delta is the final objective and extraction point once all objectives are complete and the Charlie detainee is secured for RT-7.
