Operation Antidote

Black-kit paratrooper raid after sundown to seize sealed Polonium-210 vials, interrogate the buyer, eliminate ZAHAR and Sentinel principals, and locate the clandestine lab before the cargo disappears under alert.

Departure
Erewhon after sundown
Insertion
HAHO to nearby abandoned site
Uniform
Black kits
Follow-On Intel
Hogweed Station, Fen Bog

Situation

An undercover source in Whalers Bay reports Sentinel is selling sealed Polonium-210 vials to a mysterious buyer arriving tonight. SIGINT ties the transaction to special Sentinel unit ZAHAR.

The source has gone silent and may be held at one of the target sites. He may still have in-person intelligence, so proceed carefully and search for him.

Execution

HAHO into the AO, move site-to-site without triggering radio alarms, and immediately recover the vials if compromised because Sentinel can relocate them within minutes.

  • AlphaWind Farm W02: kill Russ Lawson and the two aides managing cargo movement.
  • Bravo / CharlieInterrogate the buyer at Whalers Bay Airfield, then secure Fanny Bay Port and obtain depot access from the ZAHAR commander.
  • DeltaClear Toxic Storage Depot, verify Polonium-210 in the secure room, call CBRN, and debrief on-site.

Alert Risk

If a radio operator detects the team, assume only minutes remain before the vials are relocated.

Buyer Lead

Interrogation should reveal Russian-contractor ties and a telephone number before the buyer is eliminated and hidden.

Optional Site

If time permits, investigate Anchor Point Station north of the route for extra intelligence.
