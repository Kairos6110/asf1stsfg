Operation Vandal

QRF VANDAL will strike a hostile drone-production corridor in Restricted Area 01. The mission starts with locating the missing Raven 3 recon team, then shutting down the nearby UAV relay before finishing on Assembly Hall Omega 01, where lethal AAMON land drones are being mass assembled for wider deployment against allied territory.

Launch Site
FOB BANDIT, South Cape Station
Step-Off
2115 hours
Insertion
HALO to the IP
Loadout
Black kits with demolition specialist

Situation

Enemy forces are accelerating land-drone production on Auroa, massing lethal AAMON platforms to dominate disputed areas while allied rebels and Skell Tech field personnel take mounting losses. At 2035, Raven 3 disappeared near the production corridor, and their last location now sits under drone guard.

The immediate operational logic is clear: recover the recon team if possible, degrade enemy ISR by taking down the UAV relay, and then sabotage the assembly hall driving the wider AAMON surge before the site can disperse output or hide surviving civilians inside the complex.

Execution

  1. Insert HALO onto the IP from FOB BANDIT and patrol on foot through hostile hotspot terrain toward the first contact point.
  2. Alpha Neutralize the AAMON perimeter around Raven 3’s last known grid and re-establish contact with the recon team.
  3. Bravo Take Drone Station W161 and deactivate the UAV communications relay to collapse enemy ISR and strike coordination.
  4. Charlie Secure Assembly Hall Omega 01, account for any civilians on site, and sabotage the land-drone production facility before returning or using the on-site helicopter.

Priority Intel

Alpha is not just a rescue run. Raven 3’s position is ringed by AAMON drones, so the recovery only works if the perimeter is decisively stripped first. The drone threat is the main obstacle, not the terrain.

Bravo directly shapes Charlie. Cutting the W161 relay significantly reduces hostile ISR and strike capability before the team commits to the assembly hall. If time remains after the main chain, nearby targets of opportunity are already cleared and the on-site helicopter can support a faster RTB.

Mission End State

Raven 3 is recovered or accounted for, the UAV relay is offline, Assembly Hall Omega 01 is sabotaged, surviving civilians are protected, and enemy AAMON land-drone production in the sector is broken hard enough to relieve immediate pressure on allied fighters and Skell personnel.

Alpha

Raven 3 Contact Point

Clear the drone-guarded perimeter at grid Yankee Echo 35642 and make contact with Raven 3 at their last known position.

Bravo

Drone Station W161

Seize the station and shut down the UAV comms relay to cut enemy ISR coverage and reduce their ability to coordinate strikes.

Charlie

Secure the production site, protect any civilians still inside, and sabotage the hall responsible for assembling lethal AAMON land drones.
