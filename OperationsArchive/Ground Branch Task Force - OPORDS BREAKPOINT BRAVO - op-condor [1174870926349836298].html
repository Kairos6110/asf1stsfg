Operation Condor

A night HALO strike in black kit will investigate ORT-5's last known position, silently clear Ancient Harbor, then seize Howard Airfield, neutralize HVT FALCON near the helipad, and break the Behemoth-backed weapon stash identified after Operation Gator.

Insertion
Blackhawk HALO into northwest Sinking Country
Kit
Black night loadout with heavy firepower
Primary HVT
FALCON / John Anderson's personal pilot
End State
Debrief on site and call allied forces to establish an FOB

Situation

Post-Gator intelligence has verified the enemy unit's primary weapon stash at Howard Airfield, making the site the main objective for this strike. Before pushing the runway complex, the team must first clear Ancient Harbor to the west and investigate ORT-5's last known position after comms were lost with the recon element.

FALCON, John Anderson's personal pilot, is expected at the airfield and must be eliminated to disrupt the Wolves command structure. A Behemoth drone and reinforced ground security are defending the area, so the Charlie assault should be treated as a violent close fight.

Execution

The team inserts by Blackhawk and HALO to bypass heavy radar and air defenses, then moves to the AO under night cover in black kit. Ancient Harbor must be cleared without triggering an early alarm at Howard Airfield, since that would compromise the follow-on strike against FALCON and the weapons stockpile.

  • AlphaMove from the HALO IP to ORT-5's last known position and determine the recon team's status.
  • BravoClear Ancient Harbor silently and prevent the covert meeting there from warning the airfield.
  • CharlieSecure Howard Airfield, kill FALCON near the helipad, and neutralize the Behemoth-backed defenses.

Airfield Threat

A heavily armed Behemoth and a reinforced land drone presence are guarding Howard Airfield. Bring enough firepower to end the Charlie fight decisively.

Stealth Constraint

Bravo must be kept quiet. Any alarm raised at Ancient Harbor can harden Howard Airfield before the assault team arrives.

Friendly Unknown

ORT-5 went dark in the AO. Alpha is a welfare and recovery check before the team commits to the maritime outpost and airfield objectives.
