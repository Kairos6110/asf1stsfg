Operation Killswitch

Team Killswitch will HALO into Lake Country to steal the DTA reset module, convert the local Behemoth network to friendly control through BRN-6 at Harper Lake, and flip Sentinel’s own autonomous heavy drones into a regional force multiplier for liberation operations.

Brief
Erewhon HQ, after-sundown launch
Insertion
HALO drop from above 1000 meters into Lake Country airspace
Payload
Recover DTA reset instructions and upload them to BRN-6 at Harper Lake
Effect
Convert all Behemoths in the area to friendly target-acquisition behavior

Situation

Echelon and Jace Skell identified a route to seize control of the Behemoth drone layer in central eastern Auroa. The critical path runs through the Drones Target Acquisition program hosted at the Skell Security Assembly Hall in Lake Country, where a hidden module contains the instructions needed to reset Behemoth target-acquisition behavior.

Only top-tier Skell credentials can access that system. If the team can steal the correct credentials, download the reset package, and upload it at the BRN-6 relay in Harper Lake, the Behemoths across the region will switch from hostile control to friendly alignment and materially shift the local balance against Sentinel.

Execution

  • Phase 1 HALO into Lake Country after dark and infiltrate Skell Estate to acquire C1 access credentials.
  • Phase 2 Clear Drone Station W121 to block QRF interference, then enter Assembly Hall and pull the DTA package with the recovered RFID access.
  • Phase 3 Move to Harper Lake, defeat the Behemoth controlling the relay network, upload the reset module to BRN-6, confirm remote execution, and call extraction.

Access Constraint

The whole mission hinges on credential theft. Without the C1 RFID path, the DTA module cannot be reached and the Behemoth conversion never happens.
