Operation Red Queen

HALO from the Blackhawk at sundown, move in light black kits, restore Viper 04 comms, clear Checkpoint Viper Golf, raid Skell Foundation Head Office, recover the aircraft at Auroa Airport, then deliver it to Outcast allies at PLZ Watcher.

Insertion
Blackhawk launch from HQ, HALO to the LZ at sundown.
Loadout
Light black kits for minimal footprint in the AO.
Recon
No aerial ISR link. Mini drones are DCS-prepped and safe to use.
End State
Fly plane and helicopter in formation to PLZ Watcher, then exfil to Hideout Neptune.

Situation

Critical infrastructure is under seizure across northeast Infinity. Enemy forces are taking over the comms network, blocking a key supply route, arresting Skell Technology civilians, and occupying Auroa Airport after receiving a plane with valuable equipment.

Execution

Patrol, recon Alpha and Bravo, then flow through the full objective chain. Land undetected, conduct ground reconnaissance on the first two targets, clear each site in sequence, recover the plane at Delta, and transfer it to Outcast personnel at PLZ Watcher with a two-and-two formation split across aircraft and helicopter.

Top Priority

Restore comms and civilian security. Viper 04 must be reconnected to local teams, and civilians at Skell Foundation Head Office must be protected during the raid.

Route Interdiction

Checkpoint Viper Golf must reopen. Clear the roadblock and intercept any enemy vehicles encountered on the MSR.

Targets Of Opportunity

Authorized if mission tempo holds. Additional objectives can be hit as long as they do not compromise the four primary tasks or the aircraft delivery.

Objective Alpha

Control Station Viper 04

Secure the site and reestablish the local communications link.

Objective Bravo

Checkpoint Viper Golf

Clear the checkpoint, reopen the supply corridor, and intercept enemy traffic.

Objective Charlie

Skell Foundation Head Office

Exploit the office for intelligence and neutralize hostile forces threatening civilians.

Objective Delta

Auroa Airport

Secure the airfield, recover the plane, and depart toward PLZ Watcher.
