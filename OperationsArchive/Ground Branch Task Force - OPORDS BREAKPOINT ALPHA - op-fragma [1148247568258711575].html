Operation Fragma

Foot-move from Pleasant Island Bivouac near sunset across the Liberty and Restricted Area 01 border, disrupt the dam-breach plot, investigate drone transport and prisoner abuse, and exfil to FOB Bandit for debrief with the Outcast POC.

Mode
Conquest Mode On
Insertion
Pleasant Island Bivouac / sunset foot patrol
AO
Liberty / Restricted Area 01 border region
Exfil
FOB Bandit, East Liberty Ancient Fort

Situation

A rogue Sentinel unit working with Bodarks plans to breach Jace Skell Dam, risking downstream devastation to the Auroa Solar Power Plant and potentially threatening Auroa Parliament.

Intel also indicates Bodarks are moving lethal drones through Outpost Red Fox and may be holding prisoners there as live test subjects at the Training Center, while the dam unit prepares to target Outcast leadership.

Execution

Move on foot from Pleasant Island Bivouac, perform all reconnaissance from the ground because hostile air defense blocks aerial ISR, and watch for Outcast patrols in the AO.

  • Dam PlotInvestigate and disrupt the rogue unit's plan around Jace Skell Dam before a breach can be triggered.
  • Red Fox / Training CenterInterdict lethal-drone transport, locate prisoners, and confirm whether live-target testing is underway.
  • Bandit DebriefCollect any documents tied to attacks on Outcast leadership and deliver the findings to the POC at FOB Bandit.

No Aerial Recon

Hostile air-defense coverage means aerial reconnaissance is not feasible. Expect to build the picture from ground movement and close site exploitation.

Friendly Friction

Outcast patrols may be active in the AO. Maintain PID and avoid blue-on-blue contact while moving between objectives.

Prisoner Urgency

If detainees are found at Red Fox or the Training Center, protecting them takes priority over secondary target exploitation.

Objective Alpha

Jace Skell Dam

Stop the planned breach, clear the rogue detachment, and recover any intel on the floodwave operation.

Objective Bravo

Outpost Red Fox

Cut the Bodark drone-transport route, search for prisoners, and gather evidence on the logistics chain.

Objective Charlie

Training Center

Confirm or disrupt lethal-drone live-target testing and secure any captives or operational plans.

Objective Delta

FOB Bandit

Exfil to East Liberty Ancient Fort and debrief the Outcast point of contact with all recovered intel.
