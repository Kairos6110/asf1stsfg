Operation Excalibur

A rapid black-PPE strike package will launch from HQ aboard an Overseer helicopter to clear a four-part crisis across the AO: secure the World Seedvault chemical site and capture chemist HVT COLOMBO, recover a MANPADS prototype at Control Station Viper 03, prevent the assassination of an Auroan diplomat at Checkpoint Viper Bravo, and seize a high-value shipment at New Stirling Freight Yard for convoy delivery to Ancient Village.

Launch
1900 from HQ via Overseer helicopter
Mission Profile
Rapid multi-target strike with hazmat, HVT, EOD, and convoy tasks
Critical Equipment
Black hazmat PPE, gas masks, and pistols for Seedvault entry
End State
All targets secured, shipment delivered to Ancient Village, then RTB

Situation

ZULU 3 reconnaissance has confirmed four linked threats inside the area of operations. Hostile forces have converted the World Seedvault into a staging point for an unidentified chemical weapon. Control Station Viper 03 recently received a MANPADS prototype now locked inside a biometric armory. At Checkpoint Viper Bravo, Sentinel is preparing a false-flag bridge blast meant to kill an Auroan diplomat and blame the Outcasts. At the same time, undercover points of contact at New Stirling Freight Yard have identified a high-value shipment awaiting transfer by rail.

The mission therefore mixes speed with precision. The team must move fast enough to preserve the diplomat and shipment while still managing the hazardous chemical site and capturing or eliminating key enemy operators tied to the wider network.

Execution

The package inserts rapidly by Overseer helicopter, then works the objective chain in sequence. Alpha is a hazmat-restricted capture site. Bravo is a precision assault tied to biometric access and a MANPADS recovery. Charlie is an EOD intervention to stop a false-flag assassination. Delta finishes with shipment capture and convoy movement to Ancient Village, where Bravo Team will recover the cargo after handoff.

  • Phase 1Secure the World Seedvault, capture COLOMBO alive, and call in the CBRN team for analysis and interrogation support.
  • Phase 2Assault Control Station Viper 03, stop HVT MONK from escaping, and recover the MANPADS prototype from the biometric armory.
  • Phase 3Conduct EOD at Checkpoint Viper Bravo to prevent the diplomat convoy ambush and preserve the bridge.
  • Phase 4Secure the New Stirling shipment, convoy it to the makeshift dock at Ancient Village, hand it to follow-on allies, and return to base.

Hazmat Control

The World Seedvault must be treated as a live chemical-threat site. Gas masks and black hazmat PPE are mandatory there, and pistols-only handling is the safer weapons posture inside the confined contamination risk area.

HVT Package

COLOMBO, identified as Benjamin Hayes, is the main chemist overseeing the Seedvault lab and must be taken alive. MONK, identified as Richard Turner, controls biometric access to the Viper 03 armory and cannot be allowed to escape.

Diplomatic Risk

Sentinel intends to stage the bridge strike at Viper Bravo as if it were an Outcast attack, then present itself as the rescue force. The EOD phase is therefore strategically important beyond simply saving the convoy.

Alpha

World Seedvault

Infiltrate and secure the site using gas masks, hazmat PPE, and pistols-only handling where possible. Capture HVT COLOMBO alive, hold him in place, and radio the CBRN team to take over substance analysis and interrogation before moving on.

Bravo

Control Station Viper 03

Conduct a precision assault on the station, eliminate or detain HVT MONK before he escapes, and use his biometric access to recover the MANPADS prototype from the secured armory.

Charlie

Checkpoint Viper Bravo

Clear the bridge area and perform EOD on Sentinel's demolition setup to stop the planned assassination of the Auroan diplomat traveling through in a night convoy.

Delta

New Stirling Freight Yard to Ancient Village

Recover the supply truck carrying the high-value shipment, secure the freight yard, and convoy the package to the makeshift dock at Ancient Village where Bravo Team will recover it after mission completion.

Tacmap

AO overview tying together the Seedvault chemical site, Viper 03 assault, bridge EOD stop, freight-yard truck seizure, and the final Ancient Village dock handoff before RTB.
