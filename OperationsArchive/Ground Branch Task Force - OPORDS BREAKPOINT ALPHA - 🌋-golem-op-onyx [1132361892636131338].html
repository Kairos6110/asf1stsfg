Operation Onyx

A black-kit ground patrol from Sector 2 Eastern Bivouac will investigate Project Nebiros on Golem Island, unlock and copy data from the Missile Site Ruins server, recover the MANPADS prototype from Forgotten Sanctuary, sweep the Testing Zone, and demolish Ancient Harbor's weapons depot.

Launch Point
Sector 2 Eastern Bivouac near Missile Site Ruins
Step-Off
Sundown / black kits
Mobility
Foot movement with OSP transport only
Core Tasking
Project Nebiros intelligence and MANPADS prototype recovery

Situation

Documents recovered from Outpost Green Viper partially decrypted into a Project Nebiros lead: a Wolves unit on Golem Island appears to be testing next-generation weapons there. The intel picture is incomplete, but it is sufficient to justify an urgent ground investigation.

The mission is to collect more Nebiros data, recover a unique MANPADS prototype from Forgotten Sanctuary, and cripple any active weapons infrastructure found during the sweep.

Execution

Depart from Sector 2 Eastern Bivouac near Missile Site Ruins at sundown, stay in black kit, and move on foot unless transport can be procured on site. Tactical jammers deny drone support, so all recon must be done by eyes and binoculars unless a jammer source is located and disabled.

  • AlphaReboot four security nodes at Missile Site Ruins, breach the underground server, copy all data, then demo the server.
  • Bravo / CharlieRecover the MANPADS prototype at Forgotten Sanctuary and investigate the Golem Island Testing Zone for targets of opportunity.
  • DeltaSeed demolition across Ancient Harbor's ammunition and weapons caches, exfil, detonate, and RTB by watercraft.

Jammer Constraint

Tactical jammers and drone-swarm patrols block ISR and outbound signals, so expect binoculars-only reconnaissance and no friendly drone tasking.

Server Security

Do not get spotted before all four Missile Site Ruins nodes are rebooted. If alerted early, the enemy may wipe the underground server before the download completes.

Prototype Handling

The MANPADS should be stored in a secure weapon container at Forgotten Sanctuary and may require captured credentials before it can be opened and dismantled for transport.

Objective 1

Missile Site Ruins

Reboot the four lockout nodes, breach the underground server, copy all Nebiros data, and destroy the server after extraction.

Objective 2

Forgotten Sanctuary

Locate the MANPADS prototype container, obtain access if needed, dismantle the launcher, and transport it for specialist analysis.

Objective 3

Golem Island Testing Zone

Investigate the weapons test area and neutralize any valuable targets of opportunity found on site.

Objective 4

Ancient Harbor

Infiltrate the depot, plant demolition on all ammunition and weapon caches, exfil, and detonate the site.
