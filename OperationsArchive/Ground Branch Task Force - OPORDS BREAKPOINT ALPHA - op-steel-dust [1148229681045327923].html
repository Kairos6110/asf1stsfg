Operation Steel Dust

Step off from Devil Forest Bivouac under darkness, sweep southeast RA01 from Devil Forest Gold Mine through Omega R&D, Azrael crash recovery, Russel Gold Mine, Burnet Ridge, W161, and Assembly Hall Omega 1, then exfil to South Cape Station.

Insertion Point
Devil Forest Bivouac / night movement
Primary Target
Mine commander and lethal ground-drone production chain
Movement
Foot-mobile with route adjustments from on-site intel
Exfil
Request pickup or use seized transport to South Cape Station

Situation

Command has located the enemy unit reactivating Auroa's gold mines to support lethal ground-drone production. Their commander and cell remain mobile, but their presence in southeast Restricted Area 01 is confirmed.

The mission is to eliminate or capture the commander, destroy the drone production line, and exploit additional high-value targets across the AO.

Execution

Move from Devil Forest Bivouac to the first objective on foot, exploit each site in sequence, adapt the route as fresh intel appears, and finish by exiting to South Cape Station.

  • Gold MinesHit Devil Forest Gold Mine and Russel Gold Mine to find the elusive commander and suppress the mining network.
  • Drone InfrastructureSecure Omega R&D, capture W161, and destroy the Assembly Hall Omega 1 assembly line.
  • Intel RecoveryExploit the Azrael crash site and the Burnet Ridge weapons deal before exfil.

Commander Pattern

The mine commander does not stay in one location for long. Treat Devil Forest and Russel as priority capture/kill sites and update the route based on what each stop reveals.

Research Seizure

Omega R&D should be taken intact enough to secure copies of all research material before any demolition or site denial actions.

Azrael Exploit

Retrieve the black box and data storage at the crash site north of Blackburn Ridge to improve understanding of Azrael drone operations.
