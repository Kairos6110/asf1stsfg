Operation Vespa

Sentinel has consolidated control over large sections of Windy Islands and is using the region to support a black-market arms network tied to mercenary activity in the Middle East. Team Vespa will investigate the rebel camp attack, break the port supply line, disable the drone control node, seize chemical evidence and the HVT at the incineration plant, then disrupt the logistics hub at the Auroa Recycling Factory.

FOB South Cape Station
Launch 1732 brief / 1800 insertion
Insertion UH-60 Black Hawk in green kits
Final Contact JUPITER 6 after mission completion

Situation

Sentinel now controls a large share of Windy Islands, exploiting the remoteness of the terrain to sustain a covert arms operation. Reaper 3 ISR also indicates two allied rebels KIA at Site Whiskey India One, where a makeshift HUMINT and patrol camp was recently hit by Sentinel paratroopers.

The mission is designed to dismantle the network in sequence: confirm what happened at the rebel site, choke the port supply line, shut down the UAV attack node, seize remaining chemical evidence before it is burned, and break the recycling factory hub that keeps local enemy logistics moving.

Execution

  • Insert Deploy from South Cape Station by UH-60 in green kits and land on the first objective at 1800.
  • Alpha Secure the initial LZ and perimeter, then conduct site exploitation at Whiskey India One.
  • Shift Reuse the helicopter to move toward the secondary landing zone near Bravo before transitioning to movement on foot.
  • End Complete the Echo logistics denial, establish communication with JUPITER 6, and RTB with the HVT package.

Priority Intel

HVT: NOX, chemical weapons expert supervising disposal operations.

The critical evidence chain sits at the Incineration Plant. If the team arrives too late, the chemical weapons test trail is destroyed and the logistics hub at Echo can continue operating with reduced pressure.

Mission End State

Sentinel's Windy Islands supply line is disrupted, drone attacks are curtailed, chemical evidence is recovered with NOX in custody, and the recycling factory no longer functions as a viable logistics node.

Objectives

Echo

Secure key infrastructure to break the enemy logistics hub and create room for local allies to establish a foothold.
