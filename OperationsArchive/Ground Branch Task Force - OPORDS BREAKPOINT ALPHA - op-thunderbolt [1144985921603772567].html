Operation Thunderbolt

HAHO from Erewhon to Pleasant Island, capture the attack HVT alive, rescue Chloe Reynolds and all civilians, secure Project Omega research, then hit the Outpost Red Fox follow-on target while keeping the HVT controlled for transport to HQ.

Launch Site
Erewhon HQ / 2130 / black kit
Insertion
Helicopter to DZ, HAHO to LZ 500m southeast of target
Primary Task
Extract HVT alive and keep civilians unharmed
End State
Island secured, security team on site, HVT delivered to HQ

Situation

Chloe Reynolds sent a distress message from Pleasant Island in central Restricted Area 01. The site manages Project Omega, a lethal-drone research and production effort, and is currently under enemy assault.

Prevent Project Omega material from falling into hostile hands, preserve all civilians on site, and move fast. Based on Reynolds' message, enemy strength is estimated at 12+ heavily armed hostiles and there is no live Cyclops ISR support.

Execution

Deploy by helicopter from Erewhon, jump HAHO to the LZ, push to Pleasant Island with haste, PID the HVT, secure the island, and radio the security team to hold the facility and assist civilians.

  • Island AssaultReach Pleasant Island, identify the HVT, and capture him alive for interrogation.
  • Site ControlClear the island, protect all civilians, and lock down Project Omega research material.
  • Red Fox Follow-OnIf intel is found, raid Outpost Red Fox, eliminate the Wolf lieutenant, and RTB with the captive HVT.

Team Lead Note

Pick any enemy on Pleasant Island as the HVT, define his appearance and location clearly to the team, and ensure he is taken alive.

Possible Extension

One enemy may carry intel that a high-ranking Wolf lieutenant is holding a meeting at Outpost Red Fox roughly 1 km southeast of the island.

Civilian Priority

Reynolds' photo confirms civilians are being searched by Sentinel. Move quickly and control fires to avoid civilian harm.
