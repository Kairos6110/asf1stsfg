Operation Zodiac

BODARK special forces are actively hitting Restricted Area 01 while Sentinel brokers Skell technology into a growing black-market pipeline. Ground Branch will insert from Ancient Fort by helicopter, conduct a high-altitude approach west of the island, rescue trapped scientists at Omega Operations, and then roll the connected command, tunnel, and logistics nodes feeding the network.

Launch Site
Ancient Fort, East Liberty
Step-Off
2100 hours
Insertion
Helicopter HAHO west of the island
Mode
Conquest active, BODARK storyline

Situation

A scientist distress call from Restricted Area 01 indicates Omega Operations is under direct BODARK assault. Camp Ferret is functioning as a tactical operations center for the enemy push, and an underground tunnel linked to the camp is hosting a weapons exchange tied to the larger Sentinel-BODARK trade in Skell technology.

Camp Weasel anchors the network’s follow-on logistics and stronghold posture. The mission is not just a rescue. It is a deliberate dismantling of the arms chain, with HVT handling and intelligence capture required at each stage to expose the broader trade route.

Execution

  1. Insert Launch from Ancient Fort, stay high to avoid radar, and HAHO west of the island before forming up on the ground.
  2. Alpha Hit Omega Operations, rescue civilians, neutralize the BODARK island element, and destroy every SAM blocking follow-on air support.
  3. Bravo / Charlie Clear Camp Ferret, eliminate Dmitri Ivanov, use the tunnel link to break the weapons deal, and seize the supply truck while interrogating any senior enemy present.
  4. Delta Assault Camp Weasel, capture Nolan Turner alive, secure the full stronghold, and hold on site for enhanced interrogation and final debrief.

Priority Intel

This AO contains substantial BLUFOR activity that may create temporary opportunities but cannot be treated as a stable shield. If the team wants to reduce Outcast interference and avoid accidental alerts, conquest progress should be reset before launch as briefed in the original op note.

Dmitri Ivanov at Camp Ferret is the local chief tactician and a valid kill objective. Nolan Turner at Camp Weasel is the more valuable capture target because of his logistics role inside the Wolves’ arms network.

Mission End State

Scientists are extracted from Omega Operations, enemy air-denial assets are demolished, the Ferret tunnel deal is broken, the supply truck is seized, and Camp Weasel is held with Turner in custody so the enhanced interrogation team can exploit the network in place.
