Situation

RED DRAGON 2 reports an enemy platoon on the Restricted Area 01 border and workers at Maintenance Area fitting Azrael drones with modified Amber Ruin payloads.

If the UAVs launch, resistance forces in New Sterling and across Auroa face a major chemical threat. A QRF is required immediately.

Execution

Insert at night in full CBRN gear, stay covert through Ardmore Farm and Checkpoint Fox Charlie, and avoid any alert until Maintenance Area is reached.

  • AlphaQuietly secure Ardmore Farm and interrogate the perimeter commander if possible.
  • Bravo / CharlieSecure Fox Charlie, plant the relay wiretap, then breach the barricaded Amber Ruin production facility.
  • DeltaDestroy all Azrael UAVs, Amber Ruin reserves, and local TOOs at Maintenance Area.

Stealth Constraint

An alert before reaching Maintenance Area could compromise the entire mission and allow payload relocation or launch prep.
