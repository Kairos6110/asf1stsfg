Deploy from Erewhon after 2020, land at the LZ, investigate Alpha Centauri Residences for Jonathan Mayne, trace his movement through Skell Security and Control Station Viper 04, then rescue him at Outpost Green Viper.

Situation

At 2016 hours Erewhon received a distress call reporting Jonathan Mayne's abduction from Alpha Centauri Residences. Sentinel personnel searched the residences at gunpoint and used aggressive interrogations.

HUMINT says Mayne may be coerced into working for a Wolves unit. His ST-1 access and lethal-drone ML expertise make him a VIP who must be located immediately.

Execution

Secure the residences, exploit Mayne's home lab, follow the work trail to Skell Security, then push north to Viper 04 and Outpost Green Viper.

  • ResidencesClear Alpha Centauri Residences and search for clues on Mayne's location.
  • Skell Security / Viper 04Search the Skell Transport offices, then interrogate enemies at Control Station Viper 04.
  • Green ViperSecure Outpost Green Viper, free the prisoners, call recovery, and RTB.

VIP Risk

The Wolves cannot be allowed to exploit Mayne's drone-targeting knowledge. Speed matters more than broad site exploitation.

Transfer Lead
