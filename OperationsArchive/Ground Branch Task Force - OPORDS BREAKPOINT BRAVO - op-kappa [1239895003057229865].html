Operation Kappa

QRF TRITON will helocast into Dolphin Bay and drive a rapid canal-and-street assault across Liberty City to break Sentinel’s attack on diplomatic and Skell personnel, secure the port and airport staging nodes, and recover intelligence on thirteen abducted personnel before they vanish deeper into the AO.

Brief
South Cape Station, 2100 hours
Insertion
2140 Little Bird deployment with helocast into Dolphin Bay
Crisis
Seven dead, thirteen missing, and Sentinel using Liberty Port and Liberty Airport as active staging grounds
Finish
Deliver the recovered helicopter to South Cape Station and analyze intel with BANDIT 3

Situation

Sentinel has launched a coordinated strike on Liberty City, targeting diplomatic personnel and Skell Tech employees. Seven are confirmed dead and thirteen more are missing. Intelligence indicates the enemy seized Liberty Port and Liberty Airport as staging areas to move captives to an unknown destination.

The city fight is fluid and fast-moving. The team has to destroy immediate threats, gather intelligence on the abducted personnel, and stay ready to exploit any targets of opportunity that appear while crossing the AO.

Execution

  • Phase 1 Helocast into Dolphin Bay, secure Liberty Port, and establish the first intel picture from the enemy TOC.
  • Phase 2 Use the canal west of the port to move into the city unseen and maintain tempo instead of slow building-by-building clearance.
  • Phase 3 Push from Winter Lake to Auroa Parliament and Liberty Airport, recapture the final site, then return the helicopter for exploitation with BANDIT 3.

Tempo

Speed matters more than deep room-by-room clearing. The mission is built around fast exterior dominance and rapid threat collapse to stop more killings and abductions.
