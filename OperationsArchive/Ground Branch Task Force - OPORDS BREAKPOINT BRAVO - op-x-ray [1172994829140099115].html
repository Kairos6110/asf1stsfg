Operation X-Ray

Ground Branch is tasked with dismantling an insurgent pocket spread across Camp Kodiak, the Ancient Cemetery, and the Driftwood Islets Testing Zone. The cell is stockpiling weapons, masking movement through the cemetery, and experimenting with drone-linked equipment on the islets. The operation will hit all three sectors in sequence before the network can disperse.

Launch
ASF HQ / Tier 1
Insertion
Blackhawk HAHO into Camp Kodiak
Settings
Drone presence set to Classic
Loadout Note
Heavy gunner recommended

Situation

An insurgent threat is consolidating across a compact but varied operational belt. Camp Kodiak is supporting weapons flow and command activity, the Ancient Cemetery is being used as concealment for illicit movement, and Driftwood Islets hosts testing tied to experimental equipment and hostile drone capability.

The mission favors a silent opening and disciplined progression northward through the AO. Surprise matters, but once contact is made, all hostile elements and viable targets of opportunity are cleared for engagement.

Execution

  1. Insert Launch from HQ by Blackhawk, conduct a night HAHO drop directly into Camp Kodiak, and secure the landing sector fast.
  2. Alpha Disable Camp Kodiak’s communications hub, seize shipment intelligence, and prevent HVT Lynx from escaping.
  3. Bravo Push north through the Ancient Cemetery, clear enemy presence, and recover field intelligence from bodies and site clutter.
  4. Charlie Recon the Driftwood Islets Testing Zone, identify experimental equipment before contact, recover drone circuit boards, and capture Griffin alive for return to base.

Priority Intel

Lynx at Alpha and Griffin at Charlie are roleplay HVTs, so any suitable enemy can be assigned to those identities if the team wants to preserve the original immersion brief. Griffin remains the live-capture priority because of his connection to insurgent experimental weaponry.

Charlie is not a blind assault. The team is expected to conduct a deliberate read of the testing zone first, recover drone circuit boards where possible, and then exploit the site for capability intelligence before exfiltrating with the HVT.

Mission End State

Camp Kodiak’s command and comms function is broken, the cemetery cover route is stripped clean, experimental drone hardware is collected from Driftwood Islets, and Griffin is brought back alive with enough captured intelligence to map the insurgent weapons pipeline.
