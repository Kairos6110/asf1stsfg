Deploy from Erewhon by Overseer, clear three New Argyll production sites, identify and sample the unknown chemical, capture any HVT alive, convoy the poisoned goods north to the disposal point, destroy the load with C4, and exfil from HLZ Harrier.

Situation

Homesteaders and Outcasts have been hospitalized or killed after consuming products traced to three Skell-built sites in New Argyll. Sentinel seized those facilities about a week ago.

Evidence indicates Sentinel is poisoning selected goods with an unidentified chemical and shipping them to a targeted area to quietly suppress local resistance.

Execution

Secure each facility in sequence, gather intel, load poisoned goods into a vehicle, and convoy north for C4 disposal. Capture commanders or radio operators alive when possible.

  • ALPHAClear New Argyll Winery, exploit the top-floor makeshift lab, and collect a chemical sample.
  • BRAVO / CHARLIESearch for HVTs, secure each site, collect intel, and keep loading poisoned goods into the convoy vehicle.
  • Disposal / ExfilDestroy the load at the northern disposal point, then move to HLZ Harrier on Sween Mountain.

PID Discipline

Multiple civilians are present on site. Hostiles are greenlit, but PID a visible weapon before engaging to prevent friendly-fire or collateral incidents.

HVT Handling

If a commander, radio operator, or other senior hostile is found, capture alive and extract to HQ for interrogation.

Chemical Source

At ALPHA, the unknown substance originates from a top-floor makeshift lab. Prioritize samples and documentation from that space.
