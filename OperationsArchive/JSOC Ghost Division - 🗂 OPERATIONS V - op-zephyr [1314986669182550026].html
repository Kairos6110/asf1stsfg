Operation Zephyr

Multiple high-security research facilities in North Central Liberty are tied to the GENESIS pathogen program, human experimentation, and trafficking of live test subjects. The task force must infiltrate the network, secure viral samples and research, rescue civilians where possible, and prevent any catastrophic release before the enemy can disperse or burn the evidence.

FOB South Cape Station FOB
Step-Off 1730 brief / 1800 launch
Insertion MH-6 Little Bird to Safehouse FOXTROT
Extraction On-site helo from Delta back to SCC FOB

Situation

Intelligence confirms an active bio-weapon development chain spread across several hardened compounds in North Central Liberty. The network is conducting viral manipulation, live human testing, and trafficking of detainees used as experimental subjects.

Intercepted communications identify the flagship pathogen as GENESIS, a weaponized agent with strategic-level threat potential. Zephyr is tasked with breaking the facility chain, collecting viable samples and research, and recovering trapped civilians without triggering a large-scale contamination event.

Execution

  • 1800 Launch from South Cape Station FOB with full CBRN loadout aboard MH-6 Little Bird platforms.
  • Insertion Approach low to minimize radar exposure and land one klick west of Alpha at Safehouse FOXTROT.
  • Isolation Use EMP and electronic warfare support to collapse local comms and prevent warning traffic between compounds.
  • Advance Move on foot between objectives, stay off the road network, and use Charlie Team's CCTV intercepts to confirm target activity.
  • Final At Delta, seize the on-site helicopter, package the HVT, and return directly to South Cape Station FOB for follow-on exploitation.

Priority Intel

HVT: BLACKWOOD, architect of the bio-weapon program.

BLACKWOOD was last seen at the Cryohope complex roofline with hostages and a dedicated security element. Any on-site Breacher is to be treated as part of the HVT package.

Mission End State

Viral material and research are secured, detainees are liberated where feasible, BLACKWOOD is apprehended, and the team extracts intact by captured aircraft without allowing the GENESIS program to scatter or leak.

Objectives

Alpha

Auroa Hospital

Infiltrate the hospital, secure the top-floor laboratory, and collect all viral experiments and samples currently held on site.

Bravo

Eternity Facility

Secure the compound and force access to the underground panic room, which is believed to contain critical research archives tied to the GENESIS program.

Charlie

Cryogenization Site

Rescue civilians, breach the cryogenization room, and conduct full site exploitation for evidence, materials, and follow-on target data.

Delta

Cryohope Complex

Free civilians under duress, apprehend BLACKWOOD and his detail, then transition immediately to rooftop extraction using the aircraft staged on site.

Tacmap Rundown

Consolidated reference for insertion, Alpha through Delta sequencing, and the final aircraft extraction lane tied to BLACKWOOD's capture package.
