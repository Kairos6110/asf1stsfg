Operation Eos

An aircraft loaded with weapons and critical supplies is sitting at Liberty Airport and scheduled to depart at 0500. Outcast forces urgently need the package, so the task force must split between ground and overwatch elements, seize the aircraft before takeoff, land it at PLZ Coral, and tag it for recovery by the Outcast team on standby.

Briefing South Cape Station, Windy Islands
Target Liberty Airport aircraft
Team Split Alpha Ground / Bravo Overwatch
Delivery PLZ Coral with COSPAS-SARSAT tag

Situation

Outcast Command has confirmed an aircraft loaded with weapons and valuable supplies is staged at Liberty Airport on Auroa's south coast. The aircraft is expected to depart at 0500, which leaves a narrow window to seize it before Sentinel can move the cargo out of reach.

The resource package is significant enough to affect regional resistance operations, so speed matters. The team must hit the airport hard enough to secure the plane intact, then move it to a safe landing area where the Outcast recovery element can take over.

Execution

  • Alpha Ground team infil by RHIB from the south and push toward the airport objective by sea approach.
  • Bravo Overwatch team departs by helicopter, conducts a long-range HAHO, lands east of the airport, and establishes an OP atop the ATC tower.
  • Assault Coordinate both elements to break airport security, recover the aircraft, and keep the target flyable.
  • Recovery Land at PLZ Coral, tag the aircraft with the modified beacon, then radio in the Outcast recovery team.
  • Debrief Return to South Cape Station after handoff is complete.

Objective Chain

  • 1 Alpha reaches the south IP by sea while Bravo HAHOs to the east IP and sets the tower OP.
  • 2 Coordinate the assault to recover the target aircraft.
  • 3 Land the aircraft safely at PLZ Coral.
  • 4 Tag the aircraft with the modified beacon.
  • 5 Radio the recovery team.
  • 6 Return to South Cape Station for debrief.

Priority Intel

Departure deadline: 0500 local at Liberty Airport.

The mission fails strategically if the aircraft leaves the ground before capture. Bravo's ATC tower OP and Alpha's sea infiltration are designed to keep early warning low while preserving a synchronized strike.

Mission End State

The aircraft is seized intact, landed safely at PLZ Coral, tagged for friendly recovery, and transferred to the Outcast package before the team returns to South Cape Station.
