Operation Charger

Raven 5 ISR has fixed a hostile network operating across the south New Argyll border belt. Skell Estate is functioning as the regional command post, Long Valley Residences is being used as a communications and intelligence hide, and the brewery has been converted into a weapons and IED supply site. Ground Branch will move under darkness, break the cell in sequence, and stop any follow-on strikes against nearby civilian infrastructure.

Situation

The target cluster forms a compact command, intel, and logistics chain inside south New Argyll. Skell Estate is directing the network, Long Valley Residences is shielding key operators among civilians, and the brewery is storing weapons while supporting improvised explosive device production.

The mission requires a controlled sweep across all three sites to collapse the cell before it can launch further attacks. Tactical surprise matters because the objectives sit close enough together that a noisy breach at one can destabilize the rest of the operation.

Execution

  1. Insert Depart Erewhon by technical and stage north of Skell Estate under darkness.
  2. Alpha Secure Skell Estate and eliminate the enemy command center before the network can react.
  3. Bravo Push into Long Valley Residences, neutralize operators, and seize portable communications devices.
  4. Charlie Raid the brewery, capture BLACKBEARD, mark all IED and supply caches for EOD, then deliver the HVT to Brown Homestead.

Priority Intel

Long Valley is a civilian-covered site, so visual discrimination and disciplined target identification matter. Portable electronic devices at Bravo are collection priority and should be bagged intact for exploitation after the raid.

BLACKBEARD is the designated demolition specialist at the brewery. Any enemy on site may be selected to stand in as the HVT, but the handoff must still support the interrogation narrative and preserve the mission finish.

Mission End State

Enemy command, communications, and supply activity across south New Argyll are dismantled; the HVT is transferred alive to BANDIT 2; explosive stockpiles are flagged for follow-on EOD action; and Ground Branch returns to base with the cell broken before it can strike civilian infrastructure.
