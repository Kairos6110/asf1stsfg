Operation Iron Nest

Enemy forces have overrun the Skell Foundation Head Office and are actively hunting a C1 UGV engineer while sweeping civilians at gunpoint. Team Iron Nest must recover any surviving engineer, secure the underground research lab before sensitive prototypes are seized, then move on the Infinity transport hub to capture the enemy pilot known as Horizon and extract both the HVT and target helicopter to Summers Homestead.

HQ Erewhon HQ
Step-Off 1947 hours
Insertion Overseer helo, touch-and-go quick insert
Delivery Summers Homestead via captured helicopter

Situation

Bandit 2-4 reports that the Skell Foundation Head Office has been overrun and enemy squads are actively searching for a C1 UGV engineer while interrogating civilians across the area. The underground research lab contains sensitive prototypes and classified material that cannot be allowed to fall into hostile hands.

A second time-sensitive opportunity exists at the Infinity transport hub, where the HVT code-named Horizon, an enemy pilot with detailed knowledge of radar coverage and aerial operations, is present on site. Capturing Horizon intact offers immediate exploitation value for the wider AO.

Execution

  • Insert Lift from Erewhon near sundown in black kits and use a touch-and-go helo insertion onto the LZ.
  • Alpha Patrol southwest to the Head Office and conduct the raid with full stealth to avoid any radio alert.
  • Bravo Once Alpha is secure, move directly on Infinity transport hub before the HVT can be spooked or displaced.
  • Charlie Deliver Horizon and the captured helicopter to Bandit-3 at Summers Homestead for handoff.

Priority Intel

HVT: Horizon, enemy pilot at Infinity transport hub.

Alpha must remain quiet. Any radio alarm from the Head Office risks losing Horizon and the aircraft before the team can transition to Bravo.

Mission End State

The Head Office and underground lab are secured, any surviving engineer is recovered, Horizon is captured, the enemy helicopter is seized, and both packages are delivered intact to Summers Homestead.
