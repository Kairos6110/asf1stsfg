Operation Sifted Lover

Fireteam LOVER deploys from Erewhon HQ for a HALO insertion into Restricted Area 01 to kill OUTLAW at the Training Center without tripping a radio alert. Once the HVT is down and the center is secured, the team will roll through Fox Echo, Outpost Red Fox, and Freeman Residences to strip intel, destroy drone cargo, free POWs, and stop an active civilian roundup.

Start
Erewhon HQ, 1730 brief, 1905 launch
Insertion
Helicopter to HALO IP, green kits, foot patrol to target area
Primary Effect
Kill OUTLAW and retake the Training Center as a future rebel FOB
Finish
Use the on-site helicopter at Freeman for extraction after final capture

Situation

Thomas Blackwell, codename OUTLAW, serves as the lead instructor shaping enemy fighters at the Training Center in Restricted Area 01. He is tied to executions at black sites and labor camps, and is suspected of directing the earlier assault on Auroa Parliament that killed fifteen civilians.

His reputation is part of Sentinel's recruiting image. Removing him damages both leadership credibility and morale, while taking the Training Center intact gives allied rebels a valuable base for future force development.

Execution

  • Phase 1 Deploy from Erewhon by helicopter, conduct the HALO jump onto the marked IP near the waterfall and swarm launcher, then move on foot to the Training Center.
  • Phase 2 Eliminate OUTLAW quietly, suppress any radio alert, and secure the Training Center with minimal infrastructure damage to preserve it for later rebel use.
  • Phase 3 Sweep Fox Echo, Red Fox, and Freeman in sequence for intel, demolition, POW recovery, and leader capture before extracting via the helicopter on-site at the final objective.

Priority Control

The HVT kill at Alpha must happen without triggering any radio alert. If the Training Center goes loud too early, the follow-on objectives in the area risk compromise and loss of initiative.

Operational Gain

The Training Center is not just a kill box. The intent is to hold it with limited structural damage so it can be reused as a primary forward training site for allied rebel forces.

End State

OUTLAW eliminated, Fox Echo exploited, drone crates destroyed, POWs freed, Freeman civilians protected, local enemy leader captured, and the team extracted by helicopter with actionable AO intelligence.

Objectives

Alpha

Training Center

Take out OUTLAW, represented on-site by any special enemy, and secure the complex while minimizing damage to infrastructure needed for future rebel use.

Bravo

Checkpoint Fox Echo

Secure the checkpoint and gather all available intel on heavy weapon movement through the region for command exploitation.

Charlie

Outpost Red Fox

Clear the site, destroy the two Skell Tech drone crates with C4, and free the POWs confirmed to be held inside the position.

Delta

Freeman Residences

Break the ongoing civilian roundup, prevent casualties or abductions, capture the enemy leader directing the attack, and use the on-site helicopter for mission extraction.
