Operation Renewed Hope

Deploy from Erewhon, land northwest of Anti-Aircraft Ruins, rescue Julius Reilley, then roll through Sentinel Corp Land Base, the ammunition depot, and the equipment depot while standby teams move in to hold each cleared site.

Launch Site

Erewhon HQ to LZ northwest of Anti-Aircraft Ruins.

Primary Rescue

Julius Reilley, OST-6 marksman, held at Anti-Aircraft Ruins.

Depot Priority

Capture Land Base, Ammunition Depot, and Equipment Depot intact.

Air Threat

Active SAM coverage across Sinking Country. Use caution on movement and LZ timing.

Situation

Prisoner rescue and logistics recovery.

Reilley was captured after heavy border fighting and is being held at the ruins. At the same time, ongoing battles have depleted friendly ammunition and kit stocks, and CYCLOPS has identified multiple Sentinel depots that can stabilize the line if seized quickly.

Execution

Clear each objective and call in the holding force.

Insert at the northwest LZ, rescue Reilley, then move through each target site in sequence. Once a location is cleared, radio the standby teams forward so they can secure the perimeter while the assault element continues to the next objective.

Target Of Opportunity

Additional captures are greenlit.

Any reachable Sentinel target can be hit if it does not compromise the core rescue/depot sequence.

Possible Detainees

Check every site.

ISR indicates detention cells may exist at all objectives, so sweep for imprisoned allies during each clearance.

Comms Rhythm

Report site handoff cleanly.

Clearance calls should confirm all hostiles neutralized and authorize the standby element to advance and hold.
