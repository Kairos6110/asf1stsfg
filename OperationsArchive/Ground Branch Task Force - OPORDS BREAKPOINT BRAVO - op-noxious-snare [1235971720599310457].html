Operation Noxious Snare

QRF VENOM is tasked with breaking a Sentinel abduction and experimentation chain tied to Auroa Hospital. The team must secure VIP SKULL before he breaks under torture, then pivot into a follow-on search for Dr. Bloom and the remaining abductees by exploiting the intel trail through South Cape Station and Camp Viper.

Brief
Erewhon HQ, 2019 hours
Launch
2105 Little Bird departure, black kits, pilot retained for all lifts
Immediate Priority
Secure Auroa Hospital and recover SKULL from the L3 lab alive
Finish
Camp Viper exploited, then RTB Erewhon HQ for debrief with Schulz

Situation

Joint JSOC and CIA reporting has clarified the trafficking pattern on Auroa: Sentinel is conducting forced medical experiments on many of the civilians it kidnaps. Kyle and Emmet Benton, scouts working for Schulz, were taken several days ago. Kyle escaped, but Emmet, codenamed SKULL, remains held in the Auroa Hospital L3 laboratory.

Command assesses that SKULL may reveal critical resistance intelligence under torture if left in place. In parallel, SIGINT indicates other abductees, including hospital director Dr. Bloom, are being moved deeper into Sentinel custody, likely toward a black-site network elsewhere on Auroa.

Execution

  • Phase 1 After the Schulz brief, fly straight to Auroa Hospital and clear the entire site with strict PID before making contact with SKULL in the L3 laboratory.
  • Phase 2 Do not return to Erewhon after the rescue. Shift immediately to South Cape Station, meet Ito, and pivot onto the second objective chain tied to Dr. Bloom and the other abductees.
  • Phase 3 Reactivate the Ito replay flow, resupply at the bivouac, deploy at dusk, investigate Camp Viper, exploit any live intelligence, and return to Erewhon HQ for final debrief.

PID Requirement

The hospital assault sits inside a dense civilian environment. Target identification discipline is non-negotiable; the site has to be fully secured before the team interacts with SKULL.

Technical Note

Objective markers and mission reminders should stay enabled for the team to preserve the interactive chain and maintain timing and contact clarity during the replay-driven segments.

Mission End State

SKULL recovered, South Cape intelligence chain activated, Camp Viper investigated, and the trafficking network pushed toward a usable location fix for Dr. Bloom and the remaining captives.

Objectives

Alpha

Auroa Hospital

Bravo

Investigate the camp immediately on the back of the Ito handoff, exploit every actionable clue, and close the operation only after the new intel has been pulled and reported.
