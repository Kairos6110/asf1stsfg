Operation Moon Lake

Start at night from the No Man Highland crash site with only a pistol and minimal clothing, survive, scavenge civilian shelter gear, procure weapons on-site, interrogate Outpost Red Ferret, protect Auroa Weather Station, and finish at Camp Weasel.

Start Point
No Man Highland Bivouac / crash site at night
Starting Loadout
T-shirt, pants, pistol only / iron sights, no suppressor
Primary Target
Wolves lieutenant at Camp Weasel
Condition
Lost radio contact with Fireteam Mamba, beacons still active

Situation

Outcast Scout Team 5 observed dead civilians being dumped into Moon Lake from a truck near Misty Ridge. Tracking points to a Wolves lieutenant operating from Camp Weasel.

Fireteam Mamba was sent to eliminate him but was shot down by a SAM. Your team begins at the crash site with minimal gear and must recover combat capability on the move.

Execution

Stabilize from the crash, move to shelter, scavenge civilian cold-weather clothing only, then use on-site procurement at Outpost Red Ferret to arm up and push the mission forward.

  • Abandoned SiteMove west for shelter and pick up warmer civilian clothing and a beanie, but no military weapons or armor yet.
  • Outpost Red FerretInterrogate enemies, gather intel, and procure weapons, vests, headgear, radio, and mobile equipment as you clear.
  • Follow-On StrikeUse Red Ferret intel to defend Auroa Weather Station, then infiltrate Camp Weasel and eliminate the lieutenant.

Follow up Intel

Interrogation at Red Ferret should reveal an imminent attack on Auroa Weather Station and lethal-drone testing tied to Camp Weasel.

Weather Station Priority

Once Red Ferret is cleared, secure Auroa Weather Station, protect civilians, and collect any actionable intelligence before moving on Camp Weasel.

PID Requirement

After Camp Weasel is secured, photograph the HVT's body for positive identification, then return to base promptly.

Objective Alpha

Crash Site / No Man Highland

Survive the crash and move out from the bivouac with only your pistol and bare-minimum clothing.

Objective Bravo

Abandoned Site Shelter

Recover from exposure and gather civilian supplies only, especially warmer clothing and a beanie.

Objective Charlie

Outpost Red Ferret

Clear the post, interrogate enemy personnel, and procure the weapons and field gear needed to continue.

Objective Delta

Auroa Weather Station

Interdict the pending assault, safeguard all civilians, and exploit the site for additional intel.

Objective Echo

Camp Weasel

Infiltrate the camp, eliminate all hostiles, designate and kill the lieutenant HVT, and capture PID photography.
