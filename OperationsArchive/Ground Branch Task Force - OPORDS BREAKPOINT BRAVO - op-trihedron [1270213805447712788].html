Operation Trihedron

Combat Divers Team HYDRA will conduct a maritime-led assault across Sinking Country to break hostile control of offshore bunkers, seize the northern radar station for allied air management, and recapture a strategic ammunition depot without destroying the stock that local rebels need for resupply.

Launch Site
Erewhon HQ
Step-Off
1850 hours
Insertion
Blackhawk helocast to maritime IP
Extraction
HLZ KRATOS near Man Eater Slough

Situation

Enemy forces have entrenched themselves across a contested Sinking Country corridor, using offshore bunker positions to coordinate attacks, radar coverage to interfere with air movements, and an ammunition depot to sustain pressure against allied rebels on the southern border.

The operation is designed to reverse that control in phases: strip bunker leadership first, turn the radar site into an allied forward air-control point, then recapture the depot intact enough for immediate resupply support.

Execution

  1. Insert Depart Erewhon by Blackhawk, helocast at the maritime IP, and swim 500 meters to the bunker island for the opening breach.
  2. Alpha Secure Bunker Edgehod North and conduct site exploitation of the underground bunker complex.
  3. Bravo Use a watercraft to reach Radar Station North, seize the site, and notify local assets to establish a FOB for air-traffic control.
  4. Charlie Foot patrol to the ammunition depot, recapture it while preserving stock integrity, and extract any HVT found on site before moving to HLZ KRATOS.

Priority Intel

Alpha is a leadership-disruption task as much as a clearance task. Offshore bunker control is what allows the enemy to direct attacks across the sector, so the underground exploitation phase matters as much as simply owning the island surface.

Charlie requires restraint. The depot is meant to be reused by friendly forces, which means demolition is not the objective there. If an HVT appears on site, the team is cleared to pull him out with the package and fold him into the final extraction.

Mission End State

Offshore command positions are broken, Radar Station North is converted into a friendly air-management node, the ammunition depot is restored for allied resupply, and HYDRA exits via KRATOS with any captured HVT and the region materially shifted back toward local rebel control.
