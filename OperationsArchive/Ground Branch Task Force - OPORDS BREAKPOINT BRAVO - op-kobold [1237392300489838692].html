Operation Kobold

QRF HAZARD will HALO into Wild Coast to stop an imminent contamination and bombing plot against Liberty City, eliminate the forward QRF screen, destroy the explosives vehicle at the Toxic Storage Depot, and seize the Engine R&D Center to break enemy OPSEC and stand up a new FOB.

Insertion
2045 HALO deployment to the LZ with foot movement across all objectives
Threat
Heavy-metal contamination attempt on the parliament water supply plus a planned Liberty Port bombing
Finish
Establish a FOB and observation post at Engine R&D Center after server breach and reinforcements linkup

Situation

JSOC analysts and an undercover CIA maintenance source produced an actionable package on an enemy cell operating out of the Toxic Storage Depot in Mount Hodgson. Reaper 2 confirmed hostile forces are loading barrels of heavy-metal concentrate for an imminent strike designed to contaminate the Auroa parliament water supply and poison senior diplomats.

The same plot includes a follow-on bombing at Liberty Port intended to disguise both attacks as accidents. Before the depot can be hit, the nearby enemy QRF at Drone Station W031 must be silently removed. The final target is the Engine R&D Center, currently serving as the hostile TOC in Wild Coast.

Execution

  • Phase 1 HALO to the LZ from Erewhon and patrol on foot to Alpha for a quiet takedown of the QRF screen.
  • Phase 2 Secure the Toxic Storage Depot, isolate the explosives vehicle, and execute controlled demolition before the contamination or bombing plan can launch.
  • Phase 3 Seize Engine R&D Center, breach the servers, call in reinforcements, and convert the site into the new FOB and observation post.

Strategic Risk

This is a dual-event attack package. If the depot is not hit in time, the enemy can contaminate a diplomatic water supply and follow with a Liberty Port blast designed to mask attribution.
