Operation Black Fin

Night HALO maritime insertion for a six-site combat-diver sweep across northeast coastal black sites, restoring friendly control, hunting POWs, and breaking enemy sea-air denial between Moa Island and Sinking Country.

Departure
ASF HQ by helicopter at 1900
Insertion
High-altitude HALO to maritime IP
Team
Combat Divers / SCUBA capable
ISR
Cyclops grounded, mini-drones only

Situation

Enemy forces occupy six key maritime locations across the disputed northeast coastline, blocking movement between Moa Island and Sinking Country and using the sites as black-site interrogation locations for captured allies.

Command intent is to recapture the full set of nodes, re-establish friendly presence in the AO, and search every objective for POWs and exploitable material.

Execution

Fly from ASF HQ at altitude, HALO onto the maritime IP, and clear the objective chain with stealth and precision while using organic mini-drones for local reconnaissance.

  • Freedom of ActionCommand has authorized flexible handling of each objective as long as recapture and POW search are achieved.
  • Search StandardCheck all locations for prisoners and sensitive intelligence.
  • ReturnComplete Foxtrot at Ancient Village and RTB from there.

No Air ISR

Cyclops is unavailable, so every site entry depends on mini-drone reconnaissance and team-level stealth.

HVT Note

At Camp Tiger, the target is any Wolf encountered in the underground bunker.

Kit
