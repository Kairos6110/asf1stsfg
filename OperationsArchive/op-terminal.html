Operation Terminal

Cobblestone Industries has forced control of the RX6 project and rushed fatal human trials on Auroa. Delta Team is tasked with cutting through the company leadership chain, securing key witnesses, and extracting both evidence and high-value detainees back to Echo FOB.

FOB Echo FOB
Step-Off 1800 brief / 1930 launch
Insertion Blackhawk via Pelican 1-3
Extraction Ground convoy to Erewhon

Situation

A local CIA Ground Branch asset has been working closely with journalist Marika Fairburn during her investigation into Cobblestone Industries. The company fronts as legitimate, but its record points to corruption, trafficking, narcotics movement, and direct involvement in the seizure of Skell Technology's Umbrella Nootropics research.

RX6 was designed as a PTSD treatment meant to prevent traumatic memory encoding. Cobblestone hijacked the program and rushed live trials, resulting in multiple civilian deaths. Bravo Team is assigned to secure the testing facility in Liberty, while Delta Team targets Cobblestone leadership and logistics on the ground.

Execution

  • 1930 Lift with Pelican 1-3 by Blackhawk and insert onto Ardmore Ridge.
  • Phase I Recon Site Alpha from elevation, then continue the approach on foot.
  • Phase II Capture the supply chain officer at Dunback Farm and transfer him to Bandit 2 at the abandoned site north of the farm.
  • Phase III Secure Polaris Residences, locate Marika Fairburn, and collect any additional intelligence on the Cobblestone head office.
  • Final Apprehend the Cobblestone CEO at Head Office, gather evidence, then exfil to Echo FOB using on-site vehicles. All targets of opportunity are greenlit.

Priority Intel

Witness to protect: Marika Fairburn

Last reported wearing rain boots, gray cargo pants, red plaid shirt, blue jacket, and a scarf. She has been conducting surveillance on the Cobblestone head office and may hold follow-on evidence.

Mission End State

Cobblestone's logistics chief is captured, Fairburn is recovered, the CEO is detained, and physical proof of the RX6 conspiracy is extracted to Echo FOB alongside the HVT package.
