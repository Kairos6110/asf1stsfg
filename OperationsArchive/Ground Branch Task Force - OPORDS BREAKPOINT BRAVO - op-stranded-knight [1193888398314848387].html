Operation Stranded Knight

QRF CHEROKEE will move through the Lake Country border region to recover CIA operative JAYHAWK after his Sentinel cover was burned during an investigation into a human trafficking network. The route follows the likely detention chain from a construction-site intel cache to drone support facilities, a suspected prison camp, and finally Camp Viper where the operative or his trail is expected to resolve.

Launch Site
Erewhon HQ
Insertion
Little Bird to LZ near RA01 construction site
World Params
Drones, Azrael, and helicopters on Classic
Method
Covert foot patrol across all objectives

Situation

JAYHAWK, an undercover CIA operative embedded with Sentinel to expose a trafficking ring in Lake Country, triggered his distress beacon after his cover was compromised. ISR lost the signal near Crown Mountain, which suggests immediate capture, transfer, or execution pressure.

The enemy trail links several sites that can reveal his fate: an RFID-secured construction cache, a drone maintenance hub supporting regional patrols, a prison-camp-style Ancient Fort, and Camp Viper farther north. The mission therefore runs as a deliberate exploitation chain rather than a single rescue breach.

Execution

  1. Alpha Clear the construction site, exploit the RFID-secured storage, and search the northeast three-story building for intelligence on JAYHAWK or the trafficking network.
  2. Bravo Secure Control Station Viper 01, demolish the workshop, and destroy the MURMUR drone patrol on Crown Mountain to reduce ISR pressure.
  3. Charlie Raid Ancient Fort, interrogate the camp leader, and determine whether JAYHAWK is present, recently moved, or tied to additional captives.
  4. Delta Push north to Camp Viper, liberate the camp with minimal asset damage, and return the commanding officer alive to HQ for interrogation.

Priority Intel

Alpha’s RFID-secured storage and nearby multistory building are the first real clue nodes and should be exploited thoroughly before the team outruns the evidence chain. Bravo is technically a target of opportunity, but the drone workshop and MURMUR patrol meaningfully reduce enemy overwatch over the rest of the route.

Charlie contains the key branch logic. If a prisoner spawns in the Ancient Fort cells, that prisoner is treated as JAYHAWK and indicates his asset is also at Camp Viper. If no prisoner appears, the interrogated camp leader confirms JAYHAWK was recently moved there instead. Either way, Camp Viper becomes the final decisive site.

Mission End State

The trafficking-ring infrastructure is exposed, drone support around Crown Mountain is degraded, Ancient Fort is exploited for detainee intelligence, Camp Viper is liberated, and JAYHAWK’s status is resolved with the enemy commander taken alive for follow-on questioning back at HQ.
