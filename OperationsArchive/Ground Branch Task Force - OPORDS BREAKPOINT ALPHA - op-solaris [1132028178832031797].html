Operation Solaris

Launch QRF from South Cape Station to Auroa Solar Power Plant, rescue Clemens Auer or interrogate Sentinel for his transfer route, exploit the Detention Center prisoner logs, then recover him at Abandoned Barracks and confirm his safety to Skell.

Launch Site
South Cape Station / QRF
Primary Target
Clemens Auer, Skell level-one A.I. engineer
Initial AO
Auroa Solar Power Plant / north Liberty
Follow-On
Detention Center and Abandoned Barracks if Clemens is off-site

Situation

Clemens Auer, previously missing since Operation Citadel, has contacted Erewhon through a secure channel from Auroa Solar Power Plant while Sentinel forces interrogate workers at gunpoint.

Skell cannot afford to lose him because he has complete knowledge of the drone infrastructure. Reach the plant immediately, protect civilians, and recover Clemens or track his movement.

Execution

Helo direct to the plant, choose a practical LZ, clear Sentinel, and interrogate one hostile if Clemens is missing. If transferred, search Detention Center logs for D.I.D. 357 / Cell 4B and follow his note to Abandoned Barracks.

  • Solar Power PlantLiberate the site before civilian casualties and interrogate one Sentinel for Clemens' location.
  • Detention CenterCheck the lobby computer and prisoner logs, then search Cell 4B for the hidden note and coordinates.
  • Abandoned BarracksRetake the site, free Clemens, radio Skell for a security team, and exfil to South Cape Station.

Interrogation Cue

If Clemens is not at the plant, the captured Sentinel should reveal he was ordered to Detention Center processing for forced Stolas Mark 4 drone work.

Prisoner Log

Search for Clemens Auer / Cause of Detention: Obstructing Sentinel Operations / D.I.D. 357 / Cell 4B.
