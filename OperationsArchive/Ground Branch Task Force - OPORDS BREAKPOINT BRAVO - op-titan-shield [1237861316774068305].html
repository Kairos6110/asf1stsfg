Operation Titan Shield

QRF TITAN will counter a SEN-WOL attempt to seize Golem Island and Project Titan by stopping a pipeline sabotage, killing HVT CONDOR at the Scandium Mine, and then capturing HVT CIPHER at Camp Salamander before hostile forces can burn the island’s drone and project data infrastructure for profit.

Launch Site
Sector 1 Bivouac
Step-Off
2040 hours
Mobility
Watercraft insertion with EOD support
Finish
Debrief on site after Charlie

Situation

A hostile SEN-WOL detachment has launched a takeover attempt across Golem Island, threatening Project Titan assets, advanced UAV stocks, and any recoverable project data. Local security has already been overwhelmed, and HUMINT indicates the enemy is prepared to destroy what it cannot hold before selling the rest offshore.

The immediate enemy scheme is spread across three critical nodes: a bomb-rigged chemical pipeline, CONDOR’s activity at the Scandium Mine, and a tactical operations center at Camp Salamander where CIPHER is believed to hold the broader plan for the island.

Execution

  1. Alpha Secure the chemical pipeline, defuse the explosive package, tow it offshore in a sacrificial RHIB, and destroy it at sea while preserving the production line.
  2. Bravo Ride the remaining RHIB to the Scandium Mine, kill CONDOR near the office building, upload the kill photo to Zulu 3, and recover any operational intelligence.
  3. Charlie Move on Camp Salamander, capture CIPHER alive from the center of the site, clear the camp, interrogate the target, and gain access to enemy servers.

Priority Intel

Alpha depends on deliberate EOD handling, not brute-force clearance. The bomb must be defused and disposed of offshore using the two-RHIB method rather than detonated on the pipeline itself, since preserving infrastructure is part of the mission.

CONDOR is a kill target at Bravo, but CIPHER is the intelligence prize. Charlie only closes correctly once the area is secure enough to interrogate him and exploit the camp’s server access for the larger Golem Island picture.

Mission End State

The chemical pipeline stays intact, CONDOR is eliminated and documented, Camp Salamander is retaken, CIPHER is captured and exploited on site, and the hostile detachment loses both its operational tempo and its chance to profit from Project Titan assets.
