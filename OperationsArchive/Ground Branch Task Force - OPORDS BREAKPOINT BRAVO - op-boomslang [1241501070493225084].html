Operation Boomslang

A 2015 HALO package will recover both halves of VIPER-3 from Outpost Blue Tiger and Electric Engine Factory, destroy the lethal-drone shipment at the factory, then covertly capture HVT SULTAN at Mechanical R&D Center and move him to Safehouse VALKYRIE-6 for extract to HQ interrogation.

Situation

VIPER-3, an allied special reconnaissance team, has been split between Outpost Blue Tiger and the Electric Engine Factory after a Wolves convoy carrying the prisoners was spotted by Reaper drone over Wild Coast. Recovering both groups is the first priority, and clearing those locations will also reduce enemy pressure on friendly border forces.

The Electric Engine Factory contains a lethal-drone shipment delivered by train this morning and staged for sale to offshore Sentinel clients. At Mechanical R&D Center, Sentinel's chief bagman SULTAN is meeting a rogue Auroan diplomat and must be captured alive before that window closes.

Execution

Deploy from HQ at 2015, HALO to the LZ, and move on foot through Alpha and Bravo. After securing the factory and collecting buyer/destination intel, take a low-profile site vehicle to Charlie, capture SULTAN as covertly as conditions allow, and drive him to VALKYRIE-6 for extraction.

  • AlphaEliminate the enemy QRF at Outpost Blue Tiger and free the first half of VIPER-3.
  • BravoSecure Electric Engine Factory, free the remaining prisoners, demolish the drone truck, and search for sales-route intel.
  • CharlieCapture SULTAN at Mechanical R&D Center, secure civilians only if needed, and move him to VALKYRIE-6.

Hostage Priority

VIPER-3 recovery comes before the shipment strike or bagman grab. Both detention sites must be stabilized and cleared before the force pushes to Charlie.

Drone Shipment

At Bravo, destroy the supply truck carrying lethal drones and exploit any documents or portable electronics that identify the offshore buyer or next destination.

Capture Rule

If civilians are at risk during the SULTAN takedown, secure the site first. If not, prioritize a quiet live capture and immediate transfer to VALKYRIE-6.
