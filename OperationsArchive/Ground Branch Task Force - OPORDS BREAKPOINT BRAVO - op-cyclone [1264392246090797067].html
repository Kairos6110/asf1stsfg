Operation Cyclone

A blackhawk-borne HAHO package will step from Erewhon HQ to break up a Sentinel splinter cell planning coordinated attacks against critical ST infrastructure. The force will intercept the weapons pipeline at Howard Port, destroy the Sparrowhawk SAM site, infiltrate Camp Tiger before its servers lock down, and finish by destroying the Equipment Depot stockpiles while bringing HVT TORUS back to HQ for interrogation.

Briefing Site
Erewhon HQ at 2014 hours
Insertion Method
Blackhawk under darkness with HAHO jump into the AO
Movement Plan
Foot infiltration with maritime transfer from Alpha to Bravo
Mission Focus
Preemptive strike on logistics, air defense, command systems, and supply stockpiles

Situation

CYBINT indicates that a Sentinel splinter cell is preparing a coordinated assault on critical ST infrastructure. Four locations form the core of that network. Howard Port is being used to smuggle advanced weaponry into the region. Sparrowhawk SAM site threatens friendly air operations. Camp Tiger functions as the cell's tactical command center and barracks. The Equipment Depot stores the munitions and supplies keeping the whole effort viable.

The mission is built around neutralizing the network before the assault can be launched. Speed matters, but so does discipline: the team needs to stay stealthy long enough to seize intelligence and disrupt systems before the enemy can destroy data or lock down the key sites.

Execution

The team inserts under darkness, lands, and proceeds to Howard Port on foot. After interrogating and killing HVT NEON there, the force uses the boat to reach the island SAM objective. From there the mission pushes inland to Camp Tiger for a stealth intelligence hit before closing out at the Equipment Depot with demolition and HVT capture.

  • Phase 1Intercept the advanced-weapons shipment at Howard Port and interrogate HVT NEON before neutralizing him.
  • Phase 2Use the boat to reach the island and execute controlled demolition of the Sparrowhawk SAM site.
  • Phase 3Infiltrate Camp Tiger, pull intelligence before the servers lock down, then cripple the TOC and eliminate the remaining splinter-cell presence.
  • Phase 4Destroy the dock and warehouse stockpiles at the Equipment Depot, then capture HVT TORUS alive for return to HQ.

Priority Interrogation

NEON is the Senior Logistics Coordinator linked to the Howard Port shipment and can expose the structure of the planned hit. He should be interrogated immediately on site before termination.

Stealth Window

Camp Tiger is the most sensitive intelligence phase. Detection there risks server lockdown and loss of exploitable data, so the team should treat Charlie as a quiet penetration before shifting into destructive action.

End-State Capture

TORUS is the officer in charge of distribution at the Equipment Depot and the required live capture at the end of the mission. The depot can be destroyed, but TORUS needs to survive for interrogation back at HQ.
