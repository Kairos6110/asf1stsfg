Operation Whisper

Part two of Operation Rustle pushes onto Golem Island from Sector 2 Western Bivouac, cuts all external comms at the island satellite dish, investigates the heliport and both transit camps tied to civilian transfers, and closes on Scandium Mine to liberate the work camp.

Situation

During Operation Rustle, the team learned a special Sentinel unit was rounding up civilians in Liberty and sending them to the Scandium Mine work camp on Golem Island. The detention-center rescue prevented one transport cycle, but the island pipeline still needs to be dismantled.

The mission is to reach Golem Island, sever outside communications, trace the three main transport nodes, and then move on Scandium Mine to liberate the camp.

Execution

Step off from Sector 2 Western Bivouac near 1900, move first to the Golem Island Satellite Dish, and place demolition on the top-side main module to cut the island's external link. After that, clear the heliport, Camp Phoenix, Camp Salamander, and finally Scandium Mine.

  • DishSabotage the satellite module and isolate Golem Island.
  • Transit ChainClear the heliport, Camp Phoenix, and Camp Salamander while gathering proof of prisoner transfer routes.
  • MineClean Scandium Mine and attempt to liberate the work-camp civilians, then RTB.

Drone Saturation

Heavy drone swarms prevented a Cyclops recon flight and rule out aircraft use on the island. Treat ground-team tacmap imagery as the primary navigation reference.

Camp Salamander Access

The checkpoint comms-building computer is biometrically locked. Use an enemy to unlock it, pull the logs, then demo the main gate to sever the MSR into Sector 03.

Team Lead End-State

If no surviving workers are found at Scandium Mine, call the site and RTB. Prior ground intel indicates the camp population may already have been killed.
