Operation Neptune Spear

Team NEPTUNE will infil Fen Bog by helocast, seize a RHIB at the Ancient Village, and clear a four-site chain across Sinking Country to recover Skell Technology CLASSIFIED Report 681, protect CIA asset PILGRIM, and expose the main AZRAEL UAV base before more rebel positions are hit.

Brief
Erewhon HQ, 2031 hours
Insertion
Helocast to grid 68372, swim to Alpha, then transition to captured RHIB movement
Priority
Recover Report 681 and keep PILGRIM plus all civilians alive
Finish
Sweep Delta, exploit all intel, and extract by Lightning 1-5

Situation

Enemy UAV strikes have already inflicted heavy casualties on allied rebel positions. SOCOM analysts working with undercover CIA operative PILGRIM identified three Fen Bog and Sinking Country locations tied to the main AZRAEL UAV drone base. The decisive lead is Skell Technology CLASSIFIED Report 681, a report now moving between those sites and likely containing the base location.

PILGRIM confirmed the document exists and secured a photo of it, but could not lift it without blowing his cover. He is expected at Drone Station W051 wearing a high-visibility vest to aid PID during the Bravo clearance. The mission now is to clear the entire chain, recover the report wherever it surfaces, and prevent further UAV strikes by exposing the launch site.

Execution

  • Insertion Depart Erewhon at 2100, helocast six hundred meters southeast of Alpha, and swim into the Ancient Village for the initial foothold.
  • Mobility Shift Seize the RHIB at the dock and use it as the primary movement platform between Bravo, Charlie, and Delta.
  • Rules Move silently and fast under nighttime cover, keep PILGRIM and civilians safe, and maintain tight PID discipline before every shot.

Mission Focus

The report is the center of gravity. Any target location may be holding it temporarily, so each site requires a full search and exploitation pass before movement continues.
