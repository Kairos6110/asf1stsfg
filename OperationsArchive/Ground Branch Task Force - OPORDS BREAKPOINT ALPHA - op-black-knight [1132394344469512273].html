Operation Black Knight

Conquest-mode coastal infiltration from Owl Forest Bivouac into the MN.COPPER AO to destroy SAM coverage, retake Channels R&D Center, secure PROJECT: CLAW data, and capture ONYX for interrogation.

AO

Insertion

Extract

Situation

Bodark forces seized Channels R&D Center to steal PROJECT: CLAW research and other Skell technology data. They have reinforced the AO's air defenses enough to deny a direct aerial approach.

SIGINT indicates Bodark cyber specialist ONYX may be on site supervising the breach. Capturing him is a CIA priority because of prior government-network intrusions.

Execution

Insert by boat, clear Murmur drone coverage, demolish the Electric Grid Control SAMs and comms relay, remove the hilltop SAM, then assault the R&D center and strip the servers.

  • StartDepart Owl Forest Bivouac and acquire watercraft for the coastal approach.
  • SuppressNeutralize Murmur UAVs and destroy all marked SAM systems with C4.
  • ExploitDownload CLAW data, wipe servers, detain ONYX, and extract to HQ.

Air Denial

Multiple SAM sites and the main comms relay must be demolished before extraction is safe.

Target
