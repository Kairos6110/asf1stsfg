Operation Subzero

Fireteam POLAR will hunt the SOKOL unit across the DT. Servo mountain belt after the terrorists executed civilians at Natural Hazards Control and prepared a propaganda broadcast. The mission combines war-crimes evidence recovery, ISR degradation, HVT interrogation, and final seizure of a chemical weapons laboratory before the cell can widen its attacks.

Launch Site
Army Pass Bivouac, DT. Servo
Step-Off

Mode
Conquest enabled
Movement

Situation

The SOKOL unit, a Bodark-linked terrorist cell embedded with Sentinel, has launched a violent offensive through the mountain facilities of Auroa. ISR confirms they executed civilians at Natural Hazards Control and appear to be staging a future broadcast built around the killings and their demands.

Their occupation spreads across a support network of sites that enables atrocity footage handling, ISR coverage, network control, and chemical-weapons risk. The operation is therefore built as a rolling suppression and exploitation chain rather than a single direct-action hit.

Execution

  1. Alpha Patrol on foot to Natural Hazards Control, kill SOKOL personnel, secure the execution footage devices, and search for surviving civilians.
  2. Bravo Use a land vehicle to reach Drone Station W071, search for survivors, exploit the site, and sabotage both relay antennas.
  3. Charlie Fly to the Charlie LZ, eliminate the remaining SOKOL takeover element at Auroa Intranet Control, and interrogate RAZOR plus his personal guard on site.
  4. Delta Fly to the Delta LZ, secure Hemlock Station, and hold for OXIDE to take over the chemical weapons laboratory before debriefing on site.

Priority Intel

Alpha is both an assault and an evidence-recovery task. The devices containing execution footage must survive the fight so the war-crimes package can be exploited later. Survivor search is not optional if civilians remain alive inside the objective area.

Charlie carries the key interrogation target set. RAZOR, identified on the right, and his left-side personal guard both need to be apprehended, questioned about additional planned attacks, and then left on site per the original brief.

Mission End State

SOKOL’s occupied facilities are cleared, their propaganda footage and network access chain are seized, their ISR posture is reduced, RAZOR’s cell is broken, and Hemlock Station is transferred to the incoming CBRN team with the area stable enough to end the operation in place.
