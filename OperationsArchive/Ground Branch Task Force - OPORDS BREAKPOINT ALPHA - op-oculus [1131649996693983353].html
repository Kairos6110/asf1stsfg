Situation

Tristan Gottlieb at Outcast TOC intercepted chatter about a mystery shipment arriving from Golem Island by armed patrol boat and being received at Ancient Harbour in northwest Wild Coast.

The shipment is now held at Storage Depot in Long Valley. Nearby drone factories suggest the crate likely contains critical classified components tied to a larger program.

Execution

Insert quietly by water, move from the coast to ALPHA, hand Ancient Harbour to Outcasts once secure, then patrol to BRAVO and tag the target crate without alerting the enemy.

  • HelocastDrop behind the island to avoid detection and link up with the boat.
  • ALPHASecure Ancient Harbour, search for shipment clues, and call in Outcasts to take over.
  • BRAVOCovertly place a tracking beacon on the main-warehouse crate, then move to HLZ Grizzly.

Do Not Alert BRAVO

The Storage Depot task is covert beacon placement. Avoid triggering alarms or firefights that could cause the crate to be moved early.

Ancient Harbour Clues

Search the port thoroughly before pushing inland. The shipment's handling trail at ALPHA may reveal which factory or program BRAVO is feeding.

Follow-On
