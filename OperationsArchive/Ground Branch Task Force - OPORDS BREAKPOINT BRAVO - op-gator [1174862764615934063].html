Operation Gator

QRF NOX will launch from Erewhon HQ to stop a Sentinel cell known as the Vanguard before it can release anthrax into the Diamond River water system at dawn. The package must secure the river infrastructure, confirm the distribution line remains uncontaminated, seize the anthrax production lab, and capture the Vanguard leader for return to HQ.

Briefing Site
Erewhon HQ at 2044 hours
Area of Operations
Fen Bog and the Diamond River corridor
Insertion Method
Little Bird insertion southeast of Alpha before foot movement
Required Kit
CBRN loadout with gas masks for volatile anthrax exposure risk

Situation

A renegade Sentinel terrorist cell referred to in intercepted communications as the Vanguard has seized critical infrastructure along the Diamond River, East Auroa's main drinking-water source. SIGINT indicates the cell intends to dump anthrax into the distribution system at dawn, creating a mass-casualty event for civilians and a severe ecological incident across the region.

Current ISR also places the Vanguard's leader at the anthrax-producing laboratory. The mission therefore combines immediate water-security action with a targeted HVT capture before the network can disperse or trigger contamination.

Execution

QRF NOX deploys at 2100 aboard a Little Bird and lands southeast of Alpha, then patrols on foot through the target chain. The first two sites verify that the river infrastructure remains clean while removing Sentinel support material. The third phase collapses the lab and captures the HVT, and the final phase shifts to HLZ Saladin for extraction.

  • Phase 1Secure Bluecorp Startup, destroy the weapons cache, and collect a water sample to confirm no contamination occurred.
  • Phase 2Repeat the same containment and sampling process at Andersons Farm.
  • Phase 3Hit Hogweed Station, secure the anthrax lab, capture the Vanguard leader, and call TITAN to dismantle the facility.
  • Phase 4Move to HLZ Saladin, radio the Little Bird pilot, and extract the HVT back to Erewhon HQ.

CBRN Risk

Anthrax exposure is the defining mission hazard. Gas masks and CBRN discipline are mandatory anywhere near the lab, storage materials, or contaminated infrastructure indicators.

Water Security

Bluecorp Startup and Andersons Farm both need physical security plus on-site water sampling so command can confirm the distribution system was not compromised before dawn.

End State

The operation is complete only when the infrastructure is verified clean, the anthrax lab is turned over to TITAN, and the Vanguard leader is in custody for extraction.
