Situation

A rogue Sentinel terror cell working with the Bodarks has seized Silent Mountain’s Pumping Station, the primary water source for the eastern farmlands and population. Their intent is to contaminate the system with Anthrax, creating a mass threat to wildlife, crops, and civilians.

Parallel exploitation opportunities exist across the AO. The cell leadership is holding a clandestine underground meeting at the Battery Farm, where HVT MONGOOSE (Alexei Voronin), the unit’s weapons specialist, can be captured. At the same time, BANDIT 2-6 is being held at Outpost Black Tiger, with SIGINT indicating an imminent handoff to the Bodarks for execution during a future broadcast.

Execution

  • Phase 1 HAHO to the IP in white kits, reach OP Delta, and determine whether any Outcast scouts survived the AAMON drone strike.
  • Phase 2 Secure the Pumping Station with gas masks on, contain the anthrax package, and call the decon team once the site is safe.
  • Phase 3 Capture Voronin alive at the underground Battery Farm, then assault Black Tiger, free BANDIT 2-6, and convert the position into the new FOB.

Primary Hazard

The pumping-station threat is biological, not just tactical. If containment fails, the fallout hits water, agriculture, and civilian population centers across the eastern region.
