Operation Pyroclasm

A black-kit sabotage run from the Sector 1 IP will cut the chemical feedstock behind Golem Island's lethal-drone program, disable the refinery and pipeline chain, and then hit the Prototype Workshop that shipped thermal-imaging drones into north Auroa.

Launch Point
Sector 1 IP / sundown
Loadout
Black kits
Primary Threat
Thermal-imaging lethal drones tied to Prototype Workshop
Exit
RTB to HQ for debrief after Charlie

Situation

Unprecedented thermal-imaging lethal drones have appeared in north Auroa and have already caused multiple homesteader casualties around Cape North. Tracking a shipment out of Camp Kodiak led to a Prototype Workshop on Golem Island that appears to be the source of those systems.

The workshop is supplied by a nearby Chemical Refinery through a pipeline network. The mission is to sabotage all three nodes, exploit any secondary opportunities on site, and keep allied positions safe.

Execution

Step off from the Sector 1 insertion point at sundown in black kit and focus on deliberate sabotage of Alpha, Bravo, and Charlie. ISR is degraded by heavy drone swarms and SAM coverage, so identify and exploit any additional HVTs, servers, drones, or shipping manifests encountered during the route.

  • AlphaDisable the Chemical Refinery and clear any secondary targets on site.
  • BravoCut the Chemical Pipeline connecting the refinery to the workshop.
  • CharlieNeutralize the Prototype Workshop producing the drones, then RTB.

ISR Limits

Heavy drone swarms and SAM sites are constraining overhead reconnaissance, so expect incomplete picture and validate the route from the ground.

Exploit Targets

During all three hits, check for servers, shipping manifests, drones, and HVTs that can help confirm how the workshop is sourcing and moving its product.

Supply Logic

Alpha is the chemical source, Bravo is the transfer link, and Charlie is the production node. Destroying all three should shut down the current Golem Island drone line.

Destroy or disable the workshop producing the thermal-imaging lethal drones operating in north Auroa.
