Operation Umbral

Insert west of the Underwater Cable Station by Gyps HELOCAST from Erewhon, swim in under stealth, breach both Alpha server rooms at the same time, hunt the Bravo HVT, lock down the Campus Data Farm, and reclaim Skell Foundation campus technology.

Launch Site
Erewhon
Insertion
Gyps HELOCAST west of Underwater Cable Station
Primary Method
Underwater stealth approach, speed, suppressed weapons
End State
Campus secured, AI research recovered, RTB

Situation

Enemy activity in Cape North has escalated around the Underwater Cable Station, Construction Site, Campus Data Farm, and Skell Foundation campus. The mission is to extract movement data, eliminate the HVT, deny enemy access to AI research, and recover campus technology.

Some civilians in the AO may have flipped and may be carrying concealed weapons. Treat unknowns carefully, keep the approach covert, and be ready for armed interference inside ostensibly civilian spaces.

Execution

Deploy from Erewhon by Gyps, HELOCAST west of Alpha, swim in, and use synchronized breaches to pull data from both cable-station server rooms. Roll Bravo for the HVT, continue to Charlie to secure the Data Farm, then clear Delta and recover Skell Foundation AI material.

  • AlphaBreach both Underwater Cable Station server rooms simultaneously to authenticate and download movement data.
  • BravoFind and eliminate the HVT at the Construction Site, or catch him in transit toward Charlie if the initial lead is stale.
  • Charlie / DeltaSecure the Campus Data Farm, then reclaim Skell Foundation campus and recover all research material before RTB.

Server Breach

The Alpha server rooms must be breached at the same time or the authentication path fails. Split the stack and coordinate timing before going loud inside the station.

Civilian Risk

Reports indicate some local civilians may be compromised and armed. Watch hands, screen for concealed weapons, and avoid assuming noncombatant behavior.

Loadout

Combat diver fit should stay in multicam black or full black for the underwater movement and suppressed close-in work through Alpha.

Objective Alpha

Underwater Cable Station

Enter through the cave access and pull data from both server rooms with synchronized breach timing.

Objective Bravo

Construction Site HVT

Locate and eliminate the target on site, or track him along the route toward Charlie if he is already moving.

Objective Charlie

Campus Data Farm

Secure the Data Farm and deny further enemy access. The server building is the demolition priority if hard reset is required.

Objective Delta

Skell Foundation Campus

Clear the recently arrived enemy force, secure AI research material, and hold the campus long enough to complete recovery.
