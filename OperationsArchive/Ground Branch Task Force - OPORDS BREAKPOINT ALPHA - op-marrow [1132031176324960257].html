Operation Marrow

Bigfoot departs Erewhon at 2203 in a Technical, exploits the Lima 3 barrel dump east of White Giant, ambushes Drone Station W041, tracks Stewmaker's operation to the Chem-Extraction Complex, and clears Silent Valley Mine before returning with samples and intel.

Callsign
QRF Bigfoot
Step-Off
2203 from Erewhon / Technical patrol
Primary Target
Arthur Murillo, alias Stewmaker
Collection
Barrel samples, comms intel, and kill confirmation photo

Situation

Decrypted SIGINT captured a Sentinel commander discussing body-disposal methods and confirmed Arthur Murillo, flown in from Bolivia, as the Stewmaker responsible for dissolving prisoner bodies in chemical solutions.

Thirty minutes ago Reaper observed a convoy crash east of White Giant. The spilled barrels match Stewmaker's M.O., and the enemy is regrouping at Drone Station W041 to recover the cargo.

Execution

Exploit the crash cargo first, then hit W041, follow the comms trail to the chemical site, and finish by rescuing civilians at Silent Valley Mine.

  • Lima 3Secure the barrel dump site and collect samples from multiple barrels.
  • DSW041Ambush the recovery detachment, exploit the comms building, and deliver the supply truck to the nearest abandoned Outcast site northeast.
  • Chem-Extraction / Silent ValleyEliminate Stewmaker, upload a confirmation photo, then clear Silent Valley Mine after Reaper reports civilians held there.

Tribute Tasking

This OPORD is a Ghost Recon Wildlands tribute and intentionally centers on the Stewmaker storyline and barrel-site exploitation.

Enemy Strength

Drone Station W041 ISR estimates roughly 14 hostiles. All targets of opportunity tied to civilian disappearances are greenlit.

Extraction

Return to Erewhon by requested extract or by vehicle once samples, comms intel, and Stewmaker confirmation are secured.
