Operation Cold Resolve

Deploy from Erewhon at sundown in white winter kit, land near Mathilda's Palace, eliminate the Eclipse Unit meeting and mass-grave operation, then capture Camp Black Widow, Auroa Data Farm, and Pumping Station to secure south-central Silent Mountain.

Primary Threat

Situation

After weeks of fighting, the goal is to seize south-central Silent Mountain's key structures and convert them into friendly operating bases.

Cyclops ISR identified an Eclipse Unit secret meeting at Mathilda's Palace and spotted them burying bodies in a mass-grave tunnel at the nearby cemetery.

Execution

Strike Mathilda's Palace first from the nearby LZ, call in perimeter security once each objective is captured, and continue through the infrastructure chain.

  • Mathilda's PalaceEliminate Eclipse Unit and radio CROW to hold perimeter.
  • Black Widow / Data FarmCapture Camp Black Widow for a future FOB and Auroa Data Farm for secure-channel access, then call AVALANCHE and DAGGER.
  • Pumping StationSecure the Data Farm's water-cooling link and radio LYNX to hold perimeter.

Mass Grave Tunnel
