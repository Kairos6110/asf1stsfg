Operation Wavelength

Fireteam BREAKER will HELOCAST off Hunt Beach, SCUBA to shore, switch into dry black PPE, seize three suspected radioactive components across Red Shark Outpost, Navy Fuelling Station, and Cold War Ruins, then move fast to HLZ Sparrow for WATCHER extraction.

Launch Site
South Cape Station / post-sundown
Insertion
High-altitude flight to maritime IP, HELOCAST, then SCUBA ashore
Protection
Lead plates, potassium iodide, handheld Geiger counters
Extraction
HLZ Sparrow

Situation

Intercepted traffic between Sentinel and an unknown Russian contact confirms three package deliveries to Sentinel outposts in Channels Province. The working assessment is that the packages contain radioactive components for an underground weapons program.

The mission is to recover all three components before Sentinel can assemble radioactive payloads, keeping the operation covert and minimizing exposure time once packages are in hand.

Execution

Fly at max altitude to avoid SAM and Murmur-drone coverage, HELOCAST near Hunt Beach, SCUBA to the coast, switch into dry black PPE, and move to Alpha, Bravo, and Charlie on foot. Use Geiger counters to confirm package PID at each site, then push to HLZ Sparrow for extract.

  • AlphaClear to the Red Shark Outpost comms building and seize the first component.
  • BravoRecover the second component from the Navy Fuelling Station warehouse near the second dock.
  • CharlieGrab the final package from the Cold War Ruins depot before the handoff occurs, then move to HLZ Sparrow.

Covert Status

This is a seizure mission. Avoid compromises, keep eyes on the sky for Murmur drones, and do not get pinned near SAM coverage during insertion or exfil.

ISR Quality

Cyclops was forced to RTB due to weather, so aerial imagery is weak. Local camera taps provide likely package sites, but final confirmation must come from Geiger checks.

Callsigns

Fireteam callsign is BREAKER. HQ callsign is WATCHER. Hostiles and other targets of opportunity are greenlit if the core package recovery remains uncompromised.
