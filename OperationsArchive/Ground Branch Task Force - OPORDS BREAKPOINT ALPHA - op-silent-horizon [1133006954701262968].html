Operation Silent Horizon

QRF from South Cape Station to Southern Seal Islands, insert covertly northeast of Sentinel Corp Naval Base via the lake IP, rescue the allied POC before execution, capture the Auroa Airfield helicopter pilot alive, and return both team and HVT to South Cape Station.

Situation

HUMINT confirms an allied point of contact is being held at Sentinel Corp Naval Base and faces imminent execution. At the same time, SIGINT confirms an enemy helicopter pilot with high-value personnel intelligence just arrived at Naval Base Auroa Airfield.

The mission is to free the captive POC, capture the pilot alive, and extract him to South Cape Station for interrogation.

Execution

Insert northeast of the Naval Base, penetrate quietly to the prisoner location, secure a foothold, rescue the POC, then move to Auroa Airfield and assault the pilot's position with surprise and control.

  • AlphaClear the prisoner's vicinity, liberate the allied POC, and exploit the site for actionable intel.
  • BravoInfiltrate Auroa Airfield, locate the pilot, neutralize protection, and capture him alive.
  • CharlieExtract the team and HVT to South Cape Station for handoff.

Speed Requirement

The POC is under imminent execution threat. Prioritize a clean rapid breach at Alpha before broader site exploitation.

Pilot PID

The HVT can be treated as a bagman or another special enemy type at Auroa Airfield, but he must be captured alive.

Intel Value

The pilot is believed to hold critical information on high-ranking Sentinel personnel and should be protected through exfil.
