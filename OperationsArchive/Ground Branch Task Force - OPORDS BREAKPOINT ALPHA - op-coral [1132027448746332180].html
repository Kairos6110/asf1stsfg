Operation Coral

Twin operation to OP Staghorn. Infiltrate from Seal Passage at sundown in low-profile civilian clothing, secure five South Liberty civilian areas, gather trafficking intel fast, then eliminate Eclipse Unit at Liberty Airport and RTB to South Cape Station.

Insertion
Seal Passage Bivouac / western infil
Cover
Low-profile civilian clothes, concealed armor and compact weapons
Threat
Sentinel Eclipse Unit / civilian abductions
Exfil
South Cape Station after Liberty Airport is secure

Situation

Sentinel's Eclipse unit has invaded civilian areas in South Liberty city and is abducting civilians, including teenagers, while abusing women during transport to unknown destinations.

The mission is time-sensitive: secure the targeted residential areas, locate intel on where civilians are being taken, and destroy the Liberty Airport trafficking node.

Execution

Move fast through each civilian objective, assist residents, secure each area, and collect only high-value intel rather than deep-clearing every room.

  • Residential SweepClear John Dewey, A. R. Wolff, Burgess, Friedman, and Frederic Bastiat Residences.
  • Intel PushSearch for evidence of transfer routes and destination sites while keeping pace.
  • Liberty AirportEliminate Eclipse Unit, secure the airport, then RTB to South Cape Station.

No Aerial Recon

Liberty Airport radar coverage and urgency prevent aerial ISR. Work from POC-provided ground intel and visual checks.

Low-Profile Kit

Wear plate carriers under clothing when possible. Minimal chest rigs are acceptable, but keep visible military gear to a minimum.

Concealed SMG

A sleeveless jacket can work as a low-profile weapon sling for a compact SMG during movement through civilian areas.
