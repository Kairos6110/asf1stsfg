Operation Blue Remedy

HALO from South Cape Station into Channels, knock out the Eagle SAM site, secure Step Up Startup, and lock down the extended perimeter so the AMBER RUIN antidote work can continue under friendly protection.

Briefing
South Cape Station
Insertion
Max-altitude HALO to designated LZ
Priority
Prevent any lab compromise
Authority
All perimeter TOOs greenlit

Situation

At Step Up Startup in south Channels, Skell scientists and Outcasts are racing to produce an antidote to the AMBER RUIN nerve agent. This is the only facility on Auroa with the equipment needed to finish the work.

Intel indicates a Sentinel unit has learned about the project and is moving to destroy research material and execute or capture allied personnel. Site communications are already lost.

Execution

Deploy from South Cape Station, stay at maximum altitude to avoid radar, jump into the LZ, and clear outward through the objective chain until the whole security belt is stable.

  • IngressHALO into Channels airspace and assemble at the LZ.
  • SecureNeutralize the SAM threat, retake Step Up Startup, and call Overlord.
  • ExpandCapture the power plant, checkpoint, and barracks, then RTB.

Threat

Sentinel is expected to push directly on the lab and erase antidote research. Speed and violent perimeter control are required.

Friendly

Fireteams Overlord, Dagger, Specter, and Panther are standing by to hold captured nodes once each site is secured.

ISR

Security footage confirms active antidote work in the main lab and recovering civilians in the treatment unit who will be moved to Erewhon.
