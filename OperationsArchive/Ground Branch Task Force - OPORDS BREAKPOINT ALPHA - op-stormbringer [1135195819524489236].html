Operation Stormbringer

RAZOR will HAHO northwest of Greenfield Plain under darkness, silently neutralize the Behemoth blocking maritime traffic, seize the Camp Kodiak gunship after killing the HVT, use CAS to drive north through drone patrols, and recapture the Driftwood Islets Testing Zone.

Launch Site
Erewhon / Overseer
Insertion
HAHO to LZ northwest of Greenfield Plain
Fireteam
RAZOR
Required Roles
Heavy Gunner and Demolition Expert

Situation

This is the final assault to secure Driftwood Islets, a key approach node near Golem Island. Success will mark the liberation of Darkwood Island from Sentinel occupation.

The island chain is defended by Behemoth coverage, the Camp Kodiak HVT and gunship, land and air drone patrols, and a heavily occupied testing zone with 20+ hostiles and lethal drones.

Execution

Insert silently to avoid the Behemoth's acoustic ground sensors, destroy the drone at Greenfield Plain, raid Camp Kodiak for the HVT and gunship, then split between a fast ground element and CAS element to clear north into the testing zone.

  • AlphaNeutralize the Behemoth at Greenfield Plain.
  • BravoEliminate the Camp Kodiak HVT and secure the gunship.
  • Charlie / DeltaUse CAS and ground maneuver to clear north, recapture the Driftwood Islets Testing Zone, call in the security team, and RTB.

Approach Discipline

Maintain a silent approach into Alpha so the Behemoth's acoustic sensors do not cue the site before the demolition strike lands.

Drone Threat

Expect multiple air and ground drones throughout Charlie and Delta. CAS from the captured gunship should pace the ground element's advance.

Hold Phase

Once Delta is secure, radio the security team to assume the perimeter and preserve the site while RAZOR returns to base.
