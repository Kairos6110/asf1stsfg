Situation

Radar Station North now gives visibility over north-eastern Auroa air traffic. At 20:15 a Skell Transport cargo aircraft was shot down by drone swarms over Driftwood Islets, after which three beacons began transmitting secure Skell-net coordinates from the crash zone.

Jace Skell confirms the signals are from three sealed briefcases carrying critical data. They are still transmitting their location, but Sentinel and Wolves may converge on the crash sites first if recovery lags.

Execution

Move from Muddy Pond LZ to each beacon, secure the briefcase, document the scattered container contents, upload findings to HQ, and call GRIFFIN, HUNTER, or SCALAR to recover supplies once each site is cleared.
