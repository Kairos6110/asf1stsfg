Operation Subterra

Strike Silent Valley Mine, confirm uranium with a portable near-infrared spectrometer, push into Chem-Extraction to verify and secure yellowcake, interrogate for the Maunga Nui Port container number, then seize the shipment before exfil and gear decon at Erewhon.

Launch Site
Erewhon / sunset
Transport
Overseer helicopter
Detection Tool
Portable near-infrared spectrometer
Final Site
Maunga Nui Port shipment intercept

Situation

Scott Davis, the turned Sentinel commander captured at Skell Security R&D Center, reports uranium deposits at Silent Valley Mine and yellowcake production at the Chem-Extraction Complex for black-market resale.

A shipment is staged to leave through Maunga Nui Port. Even if the material is not weapons-grade, it is a step toward dirty-bomb supply chains and the entire pipeline must be shut down.

Execution

Clear Silent Valley Mine, confirm uranium, call in a security team, then move to Chem-Extraction, mask up before entering, inspect barrels, and interrogate the high-ranking commander for shipping-container PID before the port intercept.

  • MineSanitize Silent Valley Mine and verify uranium presence with the spectrometer.
  • Complex / Control CenterConfirm yellowcake authenticity, then interrogate the commander on-site or at Mining Control Center.
  • PortIdentify the container, seize the shipment at Maunga Nui Port, and task the construction-site team to move it to safety.

Gas Mask Warning

Equip gas masks before entering Chem-Extraction. Yellowcake is not the main radiological threat at this stage, but inhalation is hazardous.

Container PID

Recover a shipping manifest or container number through interrogation. If the commander is not at Chem-Extraction, push to Mining Control Center.

Post-Op

Move back to Erewhon, debrief, and clean all gear after handling uranium-linked materials and site dust.
