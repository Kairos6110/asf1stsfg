Operation Corrosion

Deploy in greens from HQ to HLZ Salamander, search the HVT's last known trail, rescue Doctor Mason Grady at the Emergency Center, identify The Flood by the large bandage on his left arm, and extract him alive to Erewhon.

Insertion
HLZ Salamander, then foot patrol to Airship Air Station
Primary Target
The Flood / smuggler of narcotics and Class 6 hazmat
Key Asset
Doctor Mason Grady, Emergency Center
Constraint
No radio alarm to enemy TOC

Situation

Local recon finally has a lead on the enemy officer codenamed The Flood, briefly spotted in a convoy near the Fen Bog border. He is tied to narcotics and Class 6 hazardous-material smuggling across Auroa.

Doctor Mason Grady, working under duress at the Emergency Center, confirms the HVT was treated there and discharged this afternoon. Grady will provide the target's current location if he is rescued.

Execution

Search the HVT's trail, interrogate associates, rescue Grady, then move on the target location and capture The Flood alive if a current fix is obtained.

  • Alpha / BravoSearch Airship Air Station and the checkpoint for actionable intel and movement logs.
  • CharlieClear the Emergency Center, move Doctor Grady to the rally point, and obtain the HVT's current location.
  • DeltaIf Grady confirms Outpost Red Tiger, PID the arm bandage, mark the HVT, and extract him alive.

Radio Silence

If a radio alarm reaches enemy TOC, The Flood may disappear again. Prevent calls from leaving each site.

Grady Handling

Any civilian in a white lab coat inside the Emergency Center can roleplay Doctor Grady. Rescue means clear the site and direct him to the Outcast rally point.

HVT PID

Grady identifies the target by a large bandage on his left arm and places him at Outpost Red Tiger, 2.2 km northwest of the Emergency Center.
